# Merchant

>
> **FFLF image convention**: the canonical source PNG is the mouth-closed variant. The previous open-mouth laugh version is too extreme to hold across both first and last frames of a loop — the closed-mouth warm smile is the stable resting state. Open-mouth laugh / showing-teeth grin can live mid-loop as a peak expression beat, but never at t=0 or t=loop-end.

## Role
The traveling trader and supplier — jovial, well-fed, and always ready to deal. He carries his entire stock on his back and a blunderbuss for protection.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Very stocky and round — wide body, large belly, one of the heaviest characters
- **Skin**: Fair/pink with rosy cheeks and a double chin
- **Hair**: Light brown, mostly hidden beneath cap
- **Expression (canonical mouth-closed FFLF)**: Soft contented closed-mouth smile — warm, welcoming, gently amused. The salesman's resting warmth. Open-mouth laughing variant exists as a peak expression beat for use mid-loop only.
- **Eyes**: Bright blue, small and jolly, crinkled with good humor

## Costume & Equipment
- **Hat**: Yellow / mustard-gold cloth cap, small and fitted, with a single short white feather tucked in at the top
- **Headband**: Thin blue cloth band across the forehead beneath the cap
- **Tunic**: Green/teal tunic, simple and practical
- **Belt**: Wide brown leather belt with a large round red gem/clasp at center
- **Pants**: Crimson/dark red trousers
- **Boots**: Heavy brown leather boots, well-worn
- **Backpack**: Large woven basket/pack on back overflowing with goods — visible items include a bread loaf, a ham hock, grapes, and apples. The most elaborate carried item in the roster.
- **Weapon**: Ornate blunderbuss / hand cannon — wood and metal construction, held in a **two-handed grip** across the body

## Pose Description
Three-quarter view, body angled slightly toward the viewer's right, so **viewer-left of the image corresponds to the character's anatomical right** and viewer-right corresponds to anatomical left. The overflowing backpack rides on his back and is visible behind his right shoulder.

The blunderbuss is held in a **two-handed grip across the body**, barrel angled upward and pointing toward the viewer's left:
- **Right hand (anatomical) — viewer-left of image**: forward foregrip on the barrel/wood foreend
- **Left hand (anatomical) — viewer-right of image**: rear grip on the wooden stock just behind the trigger guard

Closed-mouth warm smile (canonical FFLF expression).

**Seedance authoring rule**: when writing prompts, always use the **anatomical** terms above — "right hand on the foregrip, left hand on the stock." Do not say "left hand" if you mean the viewer-left side of the image.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Ornate blunderbuss / hand cannon — wood and metal construction, held in a **two-handed grip across the body** (right hand on the forward foregrip, left hand on the rear stock; barrel angled upward toward the viewer's left).
- **Natural one-beat action**: Shoulder the blunderbuss (heft it up from waist to firing height) → brace → fire forward with a puff of smoke and muzzle flash → stocky recoil wobble that his belly and overflowing basket follow through on. Reload-gesture variant (ram rod through muzzle) is possible but probably too many beats for the hero-screen power slot.
- **Power move direction**: Proud-and-hearty gunman — wide cheerful grin held through the fire, blunderbuss shake/wobble on recoil, basket bounces. Muzzle-smoke VFX (gray/white, not green). Signature comedy beat: a satisfied jolly laugh and a gun-wave after the shot, echoing his client Victory Animation.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Medium to slow attack speed. Reload through muzzle, aim, shoot. Puff of smoke after shooting. Not a professional warrior." Silviu notes — "Uses different foods as ammo, firing rapid apples, launching AoE melon attacks, and deploying sticky honey" (food-ammo is a cute optional VFX flavor, but the core weapon action stays: aim-and-fire blunderbuss).

## Character Brief (Client)

> **Note**: Per client, the Hero Screen / Victory / Defeat animation descriptions below are **inspiration and a rough starting point only** — not a strict spec. Use them as signals for tone and intent; propose stronger ideas when they serve the character better.

**Role & Traits** — Gusto, resource merchant, sells to player. Jolly fellow, fair trader. Gives nothing away for free, but doesn't steal or cheat — will step up to defend fellow citizens.

**Hero Screen Animation** — He gives a jolly wave and then proudly shakes his gun, as if showing off his wares or defense equipment.

**Movement Style** — Medium to slow, due to weight of body, basket, and gun.

**Victory Animation** — Happily shakes his gun.

**Defeat/Death Animation** — Falls backwards on his butt, head falls down on his chest. Optional: drops gun.

**Default Attack Style** — Medium to slow attack speed. Reload animation between shots — loads gun through muzzle, aims, shoots. Puff of smoke after shooting. Not a professional warrior.
