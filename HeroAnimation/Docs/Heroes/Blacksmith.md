# Blacksmith

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Blacksmith.png`

## Role
The kingdom's craftsman and weapons maker — strong, proud, and reliable. A working-class hero defined by physical strength and honest labor.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: The most muscular character in the roster — broad shoulders, thick arms, barrel chest. Heavy and powerful.
- **Skin**: Tan with warm undertones, visible muscular definition on bare arms
- **Hair**: Completely bald on top
- **Facial hair**: Large, full brown beard with a prominent handlebar mustache — he's actively twirling one end of the mustache with his hand
- **Expression**: Proud, self-satisfied half-smile — confident in his craft
- **Eyes**: Blue, small relative to his large head, calm and steady
- **Tattoo**: Small anvil and crossed hammers tattoo visible on upper left arm

## Costume & Equipment
- **Apron**: Brown leather work apron — the defining costume piece, practical and well-worn
- **Pants**: Olive/dark green work trousers
- **Belt**: Wide brown leather belt with a large rectangular metal buckle
- **Wrist guards**: Dark brown leather wrist wraps/cuffs
- **Boots**: Brown leather work boots, heavy-soled
- **Weapon/tool**: Large blacksmith's hammer — iron head, wooden handle. Leaning on it with one hand, resting the head on the ground.

## Pose Description
Front-facing three-quarter view. Right hand twirling the tip of his mustache with a proud gesture. Left hand resting on the handle of a large hammer standing upright beside him. Stance is wide and grounded — confident and at ease.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Large blacksmith's hammer — heavy iron head, wooden handle. In canonical pose it rests upright beside him with his hand on the handle.
- **Natural one-beat action**: Heft the hammer off the ground with both hands → raise overhead (heavy anticipation, ~1s wind-up) → slam straight down. Slow, grounded, deliberate — the weight of the weapon dictates the timing.
- **Power move direction**: One clean overhead hammer smash. Ground-impact VFX (dust burst, shockwave ring). Gruff exhale/nod after the strike. No flourish, no showmanship — honest labor meeting impact. Match Wilhelm's weapon-weight timing, not Diana's snap.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Slow attack speed, medium melee range. Not a professional fighter — powerful swings of hammer." Silviu notes — summons magical anvil for buffs (ability kit — ignore for power move).

## Personality Keywords

## Animation Direction
**Stoic strongman** — quietly proud, not theatrical. Movements are slow, heavy, grounded, and direct; no flourish or showmanship. Weight and deliberate force over speed or finesse.
