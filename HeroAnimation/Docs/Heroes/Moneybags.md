# Moneybags

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Moneybags.png` | Mouth-closed variant: `Source/Heroes Stylized/mouth_closed/Moneybags.png`

## Role
The greedy financier or tax collector — cunning, flashy, and morally flexible. He's wealthy and wants everyone to know it. A lovable rogue with a dangerous edge.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Stocky and compact, slightly forward-leaning posture
- **Skin**: Tan with warm undertones
- **Hair**: Not fully visible beneath beret, dark at the edges
- **Facial hair**: Small dark goatee/soul patch
- **Expression**: Cunning, toothy grin — gold tooth visible. One eyebrow raised, calculating and devious. The most "villain-coded" expression in the roster.
- **Eyes**: Dark, slightly narrowed, sharp and assessing

## Costume & Equipment
- **Hat**: Dark red/burgundy beret with a white curved feather — rakish and tilted
- **Coat**: Navy blue double-breasted coat with prominent gold trim, gold chevron/zigzag patterns, and gold buttons
- **Undershirt**: Crimson/dark red shirt visible at the collar and beneath the coat
- **Cuffs**: Wide gold bracelet/cuff on each wrist
- **Pants**: Olive/tan trousers tucked into boots
- **Boots**: Golden/tan leather boots, polished and flashy
- **Weapon**: Ornate gold-plated flintlock pistol — held up at shoulder height, barrel pointing upward. Heavily decorated with gold filigree.
- **Accessory**: Brown money pouch/coin bag — held in the other hand, suggesting he's just collected payment

## Pose Description
Three-quarter view, leaning forward with swagger. Gold pistol held up at shoulder height in the right hand, barrel angled up. Left hand holds the money pouch at hip level. Head tilted with the calculating grin. Weight forward — aggressive confidence.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Ornate gold-plated flintlock pistol — held up at shoulder height in the right hand, barrel angled up. Secondary: brown leather coin/money pouch in the left hand — not a weapon, but central to the two-beat power move.
- **Natural two-beat action (ROSTER EXCEPTION)**: **(Beat 1)** Toss the coin bag up arrogantly and catch it with a greedy, satisfied grin. **(Beat 2)** Raise the pistol forward, aim, and fire. Moneybags is the only hero with a compound power move — client direction, 2026-04-22.
- **Power move direction**: Theatrical showmanship (he is the highest-production reference video in the roster, see tax-collector-aac-audio.mov). Slow deliberate coin-toss anticipation → catch with a flourish → swing pistol forward and fire with a gold-tinted muzzle flash and optionally coins as projectile VFX (matches Silviu's "fires coins from his weapon" note). Cunning grin → calculating aim → satisfied smirk after the shot. Feather on beret and coat flaps as secondary motion.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Medium attack speed. Reload through muzzle, aim, shoot. Puff of smoke after shooting. Not a professional warrior." Silviu notes — "Fires coins from his weapon and unleashes AoE coin attacks using his coin bag." CSV `Hero Screen Animation` — "He leans back arrogantly, tossing his coin bag into the air and catching it with a greedy, satisfied grin" (this is beat 1 of the exception).

## Character Brief (Client)

> **Note**: Per client, the Hero Screen / Victory / Defeat animation descriptions below are **inspiration and a rough starting point only** — not a strict spec. Use them as signals for tone and intent; propose stronger ideas when they serve the character better.

**Role & Traits** — Moneybags the Tax Collector. Player's tax collector. Greedy, constantly wants more money. Arrogant, makes fun of those he takes money from. Really enjoys his job.

**Hero Screen Animation** — He leans back arrogantly, tossing his coin bag into the air and catching it with a greedy, satisfied grin.

**Movement Style** — Medium speed, faster than Gusto.

**Victory Animation** — Arrogantly waves his coin bag up and down.

**Defeat/Death Animation** — Clutches his coin bag tightly as he falls to his knees, then lands on his stomach.

**Default Attack Style** — Medium attack speed. Reload animation between shots — loads gun through muzzle, aims, shoots. Puff of smoke after shooting. Not a professional warrior.
