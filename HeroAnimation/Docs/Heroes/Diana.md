# Diana

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Diana.png`

## Role
A young, scrappy warrior woman — resourceful and brave. She's a frontier fighter or ranger-type, practical and self-reliant.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Slim and athletic for the chibi style, compact proportions
- **Skin**: Fair with warm undertones, rosy cheeks
- **Hair**: Vibrant red/copper, short and tousled, swept to one side with visible volume
- **Expression**: Subtle confident smirk, one eyebrow slightly raised — sly and self-assured
- **Eyes**: Bright green, large and alert

## Costume & Equipment
- **Tunic**: Teal/turquoise knee-length tunic dress with subtle texture
- **Shoulder collar**: Gray-brown fur shoulder collar / stole around both shoulders (worn armor, body-attached). Note: prompts must use "fur shoulder collar" / "fur stole" — never "mantle" (over-reads to full cape silhouette in Seedance).
- **Arm guards**: Brown leather bracers/arm wraps
- **Belt**: Dark brown leather belt with bronze buckles, layered with a second cross-belt
- **Cape**: Teal cape matching the tunic, partially visible behind
- **Boots**: Brown leather boots with fur trim at the top, chunky soles
- **Primary weapon**: Short sword — clean silver blade with gold spherical pommel and crossguard. Held in anatomical-left hand at shoulder height.
- **Secondary weapon**: A **second matching short sword** sheathed/tucked at the right hip (gold pommel + crossguard visible at the belt). Opens the door to a **dual-wield power move concept** per Silviu notes ("Dual wielder, attacks with both swords, performs whirlwind attacks") — sword can be drawn from the hip during the power beat and re-sheathed before the loop closes (so the FFLF first/last frame still matches the canonical single-sword pose).

## Pose Description
Three-quarter view. Sword held upright at shoulder height in her **anatomical left hand** (viewer's right side of image), blade angled up and back behind her head. Her **anatomical right hand** (viewer's left side of image) rests confidently on the hip near the belt. Weight shifted slightly to one side — relaxed but ready. A **second matching short sword** is sheathed at the right hip (gold pommel + crossguard visible).

**Hand convention for prompts** (Seedance reads anatomical, not viewer-side):
- Sword in **left hand** = anatomical left = viewer's right side of image
- Hip rest hand = **right hand** = anatomical right = viewer's left side of image
- "sword stays in left hand always" / "right hand stays on hip" Constraints clauses required

**FFLF expression**: canonical PNG already has a closed-mouth confident smirk — neutral resting expression, suitable for FFLF as-is. No mouth-closed variant required.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Short sword — clean silver blade with gold spherical pommel and crossguard. Held in one hand at shoulder height in the canonical pose.
- **Natural one-beat action**: Quick graceful flourish-slash — pull back short (~0.3s), fast horizontal or diagonal slash, finish with blade raised or fist punched in the air. Fast-paced, agile, short anticipation-to-strike ratio. Silviu notes mention dual-wield whirlwind — out of scope for hero screen; single flourish carries the beat.
- **Power move direction**: One crisp flourish slash with a bright white slash-trail VFX arc (Diana is the reference gold-standard for slash trails — see StyleGuide_Animation §3). End in the canonical raised-blade pose. Passionate/hot-headed expression arc: smirk → fierce focus → triumphant grin.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Fast attack speed, long melee range. Professional fighter — stabs and slashes with sword." Silviu notes — "Dual wielder, attacks with both swords, performs whirlwind attacks" (ignore dual-wield for hero screen; source art shows single sword).
