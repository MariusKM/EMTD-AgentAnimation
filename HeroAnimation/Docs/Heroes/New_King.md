## Role
The player's primary avatar — a gentle and kind king who leads from the front. He's a strong, confident, and experienced leader — approachable and heroic, the everyman ruler.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Stocky and compact, standard chibi proportions (~2.5 heads tall)
- **Skin**: Warm peachy tan with rosy undertones — visible rosy hues on ears, nose tip, and cheeks
- **Hair**: Dark brown, medium length, swept back from the forehead, visible on sides and back beneath crown
- **Facial hair**: Full brown beard, neatly shaped, medium length
- **Expression**: Confident smile, friendly and determined — mouth open showing teeth
- **Eyes**: Brown, warm, slightly narrowed in a self-assured expression

## Costume & Equipment
- **Crown**: Gold crown with a prominent emerald gem at the center peak, three-pointed design
- **Tunic**: Olive-green medieval tunic with gold trim and emerald gem buckles/clasps down the front
- **Cape**: Maroon cape draped over both shoulders, flowing behind, fastened with emerald gem clasps at the chest
- **Belt**: Gold-accented belt with scroll pouch/satchel at the hip (tan leather)
- **Boots**: Sturdy brown leather boots, knee-height, chunky soles
- **Weapon**: Single-handed straight sword — clean silver blade, gold crossguard with emerald gems inset
- **Arm bracers**: Gold-trimmed wrist cuffs
- **Accessories**: Rolled parchment scrolls visible in belt pouch
