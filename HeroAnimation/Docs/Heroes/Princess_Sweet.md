# Princess Sweet

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Princess_Sweet.png`

## Role
The classic fairy-tale princess — kind, gentle, and genuinely sweet. She represents the idealized, innocent side of royalty. A romantic and hopeful character.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Slim and graceful, the most traditionally proportioned female character
- **Skin**: Very fair/pale porcelain with pink cheeks
- **Hair**: Golden blonde, styled in an elegant large bun/updo with loose strands framing the face
- **Expression**: Gentle, warm smile — kind and sincere, soft and inviting
- **Eyes**: Large, bright blue — the most prominent and sparkling eyes in the roster. Wide and innocent.
- **Accessories**: Small pearl drop earrings, delicate silver/crystal tiara nestled in the hair, pearl choker necklace

## Costume & Equipment
- **Tiara**: Delicate silver/crystal tiara with a blue gem center, placed subtly in the updo
- **Dress**: Flowing blue ball gown — the most elegant dress in the roster:
  - Rich cerulean/royal blue bodice, off-shoulder with short puffed sleeves
  - Multi-layered ruffled blue skirt with cascading tiers
  - White/cream underskirt visible at the hem, decorated with small blue floral embroidery
- **Jewelry**: Pearl drop earrings, pearl choker necklace at the throat
- **Held item**: Single red rose — held delicately in the right hand at chest height. The only warm-colored element, creating a focal point.

## Pose Description
Gentle three-quarter view with a slight turn of the shoulders. Right hand holds the red rose delicately at chest height, arm slightly extended. Left hand relaxed at the side with a graceful open gesture. Head tilted slightly with the kind smile — an inviting, open composition. The flowing dress creates a wide, bell-shaped silhouette at the bottom.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Single red rose held in the right hand — her signature item, no literal weapon in the canonical pose. Treated as her "weapon" for the hero-screen power move, channeling thorn-vine magic per the Silviu notes.
- **Natural one-beat action**: Lift the rose slightly forward → thorn vines coil briefly around her wrist/forearm and extend outward a short distance → vines recede and she settles back to the canonical pose with a gentle smile. Fast graceful motion underneath, but the VFX (vines) is the visible beat, not the hand movement.
- **Power move direction**: Rose-and-thorn conjure. Soft, feminine delivery — no combat read. Vines are dark green with small purple or red thorn accents (do not use pure bright green — keying risk on green chroma). Faint rose-petal drift as secondary VFX. The "hidden tomboy" in her traits can surface as a brief mischievous smile after the vines retract — single warm comedy beat, not a full character shift.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — not specified. Silviu notes — "A debuffer hero who slows and weakens enemies, and can grant a thorns effect to an allied tank. When casting, vines wrap around her hands and body, channeling thorn-themed magic" (debuff/slow = ability kit, ignore; thorn-vine conjure = the weapon action).
