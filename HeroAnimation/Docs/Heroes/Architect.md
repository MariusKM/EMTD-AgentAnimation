## Role
The master builder and engineer — scholarly, precise, and inventive. He designs the kingdom's structures with both intellect and flair, and carries himself with the quiet superiority of someone who has already solved the problem you're still working on.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Stocky and compact, standard chibi proportions
- **Skin**: Warm tan with rosy undertones
- **Hair**: Not visible (covered by turban)
- **Facial hair**: Thin dark handlebar mustache, neatly curled and waxed at the tips
- **Expression**: Closed-mouth confident smirk — lips upturned at the corners, chin lifted, eyes slightly half-lidded behind the glasses. Smug, refined, "I have already solved this." Not a wide grin, not manic.
- **Eyes**: Large, magnified behind round glasses, warm brown

## Costume & Equipment
- **Headwear**: Large red turban/wrapped head covering — the most prominent headwear in the roster. Volumous and draped, with a long red fabric tail draping down the anatomical-right side of the head (viewer's left).
- **Glasses**: Round gold-framed spectacles — a distinguishing feature
- **Tunic**: Green tunic with rolled-up sleeves, practical workwear feel
- **Harness**: Brown leather cross-body harness/suspenders over the tunic with a brass center buckle at the chest
- **Boots**: Brown leather boots, sturdy workman style
- **Weapon (right hand)**: Large silver caliper-protractor **blade** — a literal weapon whose silhouette borrows the shape of a draftsman's caliper-divider with a protractor arc along the bottom. Held forward across the body, weapon-scale (roughly the size of his torso). Polished silver with painted highlights and engraved tick marks along the protractor arc.
- **Scrolls (left arm)**: Bundle of three rolled parchment scrolls tucked under the arm — building plans and blueprints
- **Accessory**: Fully extended articulated brass folding-rule / drafting arm sticking up and out from the top-right of the turban (anatomical-left side). Conspicuous, roughly 1/3 of the head's height. Secondary-motion candidate — bobs with head moves.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Large silver **caliper-protractor blade** in the anatomical-right hand — a literal blade shaped like a draftsman's caliper-divider with a protractor arc along the bottom edge. Weapon-scale, polished silver with engraved tick marks. Bundle of three parchment scrolls/blueprints cradled in the anatomical-left arm.
- **Natural one-beat action**: Adjust glasses with the off hand (anatomical-left would have to set the scrolls aside — prefer a different anticipation), then flick the caliper-protractor blade forward in a short measuring-stab or controlled slash. The "I have measured and found you wanting" gesture.
- **Power move direction**: Refined-blade-work — the caliper-protractor blade is a real cutting weapon used with a draftsman's precision. Smug anticipation (chin lift, half-lidded look down the nose, brow arch) → controlled slash, jab, or measuring-arc swing → settle back into the smirk with a self-satisfied beat. Subtle paper-flutter or fine line-trail VFX is on the table; no magic, no broad combat heroics. Personality reads as "I have already won; this is a formality."
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Medium attack speed, short melee range. Not a professional fighter — stabs and slashes." Silviu notes — uses scrolls to cast protective spells (ability kit — ignore for power move).
