# Fat King

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Fat_king.png`

## Role
The jolly, indulgent nobleman — fabulously rich, always elegant, generous, and perpetually bored in search of entertainment. A warm comedic figure whose roundness and appetite for leisure are his defining traits. More amiable than antagonistic — a good-natured aristocrat enjoying the spoils of his station.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Very rotund and round — the second-widest character after Merchant. Pear-shaped with a **large prominent belly** that functions as his comedic focal point. His roundness is a feature, not a flaw — it radiates prosperity, leisure, and good humor.
- **Skin**: Fair/pale pink, soft and pampered-looking
- **Hair**: White curly powdered wig in an 18th-century aristocratic style — shoulder-length with elaborate curls. The most ornate hairstyle in the roster.
- **Expression**: Warm, jolly, self-satisfied smile — genuinely pleased and good-natured rather than smug. Slight contented squint, relaxed eyebrows. Reads as "enjoying himself" not "looking down at you."
- **Eyes**: Blue, small and bright, softly creased with amusement

## Costume & Equipment
- **Crown**: Ornate gold crown with a red velvet cap and blue-gemmed points — proper royal crown, perched atop the powdered wig
- **Ermine scarf**: Prominent white fur scarf/stole with dark spots draped around the neck and across shoulders — classic aristocratic ermine, a major silhouette feature. Note: describe as "ermine scarf" in prompts (never "mantle" — Seedance reads "mantle" as a full cape).
- **Coat**: Elaborate teal/turquoise jacket with extensive gold trim and embroidery. Gold arrow/chevron ornaments flank the button line on the front; diamond-grid gold pattern on the sleeves.
- **Undershirt**: Crimson red vest/waistcoat with three large gold buttons, visible beneath the coat
- **Collar**: White ruffled collar/jabot at the neck — aristocratic period detail
- **Clasp**: Large gold medallion/brooch with crown motif at the chest, anchoring the ermine scarf
- **Pants**: Crimson red puffy breeches
- **Stockings**: Short white stockings/gaiters visible between the breeches cuff and boot top
- **Boots**: Short brown boots with wide soles
- **Scepter (croissant pole-arm)**: Tall ornate golden scepter with a **large ribbed golden croissant** as the head — a curled, fluted crescent shape, absurd and unmistakably pastry-like. Twisted rope-pattern shaft. Held upright in one hand like a walking stick. The croissant head is his signature comedic weapon.

## Pose Description
Three-quarter view, standing with exaggerated posture — **belly pushed proudly forward** as the central focal point, right hand resting on hip (viewer's left side of image), left hand holding the golden croissant scepter upright (viewer's right side of image). Head tilted with the warm, jolly smile. The pose radiates prosperity, contentment, and good-natured aristocratic charm — he is enjoying himself.

> **Seedance hand convention**: Seedance interprets "left hand" / "right hand" as the **character's anatomical** hands, not the viewer's sides. The scepter is held in his **left** hand (viewer's right side of image); the **right** hand rests on his hip (viewer's left side of image). Always use this anatomical convention in prompts.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Golden croissant pole-arm scepter — tall ornate staff with a ribbed golden croissant head. Held upright in his **anatomical left** hand (viewer's right side of image).
- **Natural one-beat action**: Raise the scepter slightly with both hands and thump the croissant end down on the ground with a satisfied nod — royal proclamation cadence. Alternative: short forward stab with the croissant tip (absurd-weapon comedy beat).
- **Power move direction**: Slow, heavy, regal. One deliberate scepter thump (ground impact, wig curls and belly wobble as secondary motion) → belly-forward chuckle. Crown gleams on the impact. Keep the belly as the comedic co-star — the scepter action should cause the belly to wobble, not be separate from it. Use `sways`/`rocks` for body motion, never `rolls` (Seedance filter — see CLAUDE.md).
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Slow attack speed, medium to long melee range. Stabbing or smashing with croissant pole-arm." Silviu notes — summons a feast buffing allies (ability kit — ignore for power move).

## Personality Keywords
Jolly, rotund, generous, elegant, bored (seeking entertainment), indulgent, theatrical, warm, good-natured, self-satisfied (but amiably so)

## Animation Focus
- **The belly is the comedic centerpiece** — wobble, pats, hearty belly-laughs, belly-forward posture. Every power and idle concept should find a way to make his roundness and belly part of the beat.
- Motion style is **heavy and slow** — more rolling than walking, wobbling momentum, delayed follow-through on the belly and the wig curls.
- Expression arc leans **jovial and slightly bored** — a bored-royal sigh, a looking-around-for-entertainment glance, a sudden delighted smile when something amuses him.
