# Count Wilhelm

> Source: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Count_Wilhelm.png`

## Role
A veteran warlord and seasoned noble — imposing, experienced, and stern. He commands through intimidation and battle-hardened authority.

## Physical Description
- **Build**: Broad and stocky, heavy upper body in armor, powerfully built
- **Skin**: Weathered tan with ruddy undertones
- **Hair**: White/silver, swept back, thick and voluminous
- **Facial hair**: Large white handlebar mustache, prominent and distinctive
- **Expression**: Stern, intense glare — furrowed brow, mouth set in a firm line. The most intimidating face in the roster.
- **Eyes**: Dark, deep-set, shadowed beneath heavy brows — piercing and sharp

## Costume & Equipment
- **Armor**: Polished silver plate armor on shoulders and upper body, with visible pauldrons
- **Under-armor**: Teal/dark blue tunic visible beneath the chest plate
- **Cape**: Deep crimson/burgundy cape draped from both shoulders, falling behind to the character's anatomical-left side; reads as a static drape in the source, not a sweep
- **Necklace/collar**: Ornate thick gold chain necklace with five large faceted red/orange cabochon-cut gemstones — a mark of noble rank
- **Arm guards**: Brown leather with metal plate reinforcement
- **Boots**: Heavy brown leather boots, military style
- **Weapon**: Large ornate halberd — etched silver axe-head with decorative scrollwork and a top spike, long brown wooden haft. Held with both hands in a battle-ready grip.

## Pose Description
Facing the viewer at a strong three-quarter angle, gripping the ornate halberd with both hands diagonally across the body. Leaning forward aggressively, chin slightly lowered for an intimidating under-brow glare. Cape draped behind to the anatomical-left side.

**Hand assignment (anatomical, per Seedance convention)**: The halberd is held with the **anatomical right hand high** on the haft, gripping just below the axe-head (viewer-left side of the image). The **anatomical left hand is lower** on the haft, near the butt of the pole (viewer-right side of the image). Both hands on the weapon at the canonical pose — any single-hand beard-stroke or gesture must release one hand mid-loop and return to the two-handed grip by loop end so FFLF first/last frame matches.

**Signature VFX color**: Blue (per in-game VFX). Power-move FX should lean cool blue — blade glow, energy arc, impact burst — not gold or white. Blue is safe on green chroma.

## Weapon
- **Primary**: Ornate halberd — etched silver axe-head with top spike and decorative scrollwork, long brown wooden haft. Held with both hands diagonally across the body.
- **Natural one-beat action**: One sharp forward halberd jab (long reach) OR a short overhead swing bringing the axe-head down into frame. Professional fighter — cleaner, faster timing than Blacksmith's wind-up, but still weighty.
- **Power move direction**: Single decisive halberd action — stab forward with the spike, or short swing down with the blade. Cape sweeps back with the motion and settles. Stern glare held throughout. A dry smirk or approving nod after the strike keeps the jovial tone without undermining the intimidation.
- **Source of truth**: CSV `Default Attack Style` — "Medium attack speed, long melee range. Professional fighter — stabs and swings of halberd." Silviu notes — "Strong 2 handed tank with swipe and stab attacks."
