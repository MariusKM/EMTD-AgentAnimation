MULTI-SHOT EXAMPLE (3-shot rural cooking scene)
------------------------------------------------
Global blocks defined once. Refs used inline within each shot's Action/Visuals.

[Style]
Modern rural aesthetic, cinematic commercial quality, shot on Sony A7S3, 4K ultra-clear,
natural transparent lighting, healing ASMR feel, no historical costume drama.

[Scene]
A well-maintained modern farmhouse open kitchen. Background: lush vegetable garden,
bright sunshine.

[Character]
Modern rural creator, black long hair casually tied with a wooden hairpin, dark blue linen
outfit, clear makeup, focused and peaceful expression.

[@Refs]
@image1 = character appearance/face, @image2 = kitchen and scene environment, @audio1 = ASMR nature ambience

[Shot Details]

[00:00-00:05] Shot 1: Morning Harvest
Visuals: Extreme close-up. Morning side-backlight hits the plants.
Action: @image1's bare hands pick a bright red tomato with glistening dew drops from the vine.
Details: Razor-sharp focus on tomato fuzz and sliding water droplets. @image2 blurred in background.

[00:05-00:10] Shot 2: Craftsmanship
Visuals: @image2 indoor stove area, full of life but spotless.
Action: @image1 cuts vegetables with skilled, precise movements. Macro captures knife slicing
through ingredients, juice splattering. Cut to orange flame in earthen stove, warm and real.
Details: Non-performative naturalism. Light and shadow warm and grounded.

[00:10-00:15] Shot 3: Tranquil Moment
Visuals: Full shot into medium shot of @image2 yard table setting.
Action: @image1 sits down quietly, gently tidies a stray hair, takes a quiet bite.
Details: Steam rises slowly against backlight. Near silence — you can almost hear the wind.
