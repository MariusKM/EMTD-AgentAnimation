## Notes for this concept

- **Greed-driven expressive idle** — opens the pouch, peeks inside, closes it. Pure miserly check-on-the-money beat.
- **Pouch and pistol both stay absolutely still** (revised 2026-05-06 v2) — only the head and eyes move. The pouch stays in canonical hip-height position the entire loop. NO drawstring lifting, NO pouch lifting, NO opening of the pouch — the head simply glances down toward the pouch and a tiny inside-pouch gleam catches his eye. (Earlier versions had Seedance interpret "lift the pouch by the drawstring" as the pouch being lifted by an imaginary string, which read poorly.)
- **Closed-mouth throughout** — smirk widens fractionally on the satisfied head-nod but never opens.
- **Tiny gold sparkle from inside the pouch on the peek** — light catching the coins inside. Warm gold sparkle, never green.
- **Always-hold both props** — pouch must stay in left hand throughout (tilt and open the top, but never let go).
- **FFLF integrity** — pouch must be closed and tied at the loop start AND end; only opens briefly mid-loop.
