## Notes for this concept
- **Multi-beat narrative arc** — too-forceful flick → over-unfurl drop → startled freeze → recovery yank → composure flourish. Must enumerate as discrete timecoded beats.
- **`unfurl` not `unroll` everywhere** — the over-unfurl + re-coil is the structural beat, every reference uses `unfurl` / `over-extends` / `re-coil` per Architect P2 trap.
- **Open-mouth peak as a startled "oh!" expression** — uses the wide-grin variant's open-mouth shape but with eyes slightly wider and mouth in a small surprised round shape, not a beaming grin. Mid-loop only; FFLF stays the closed-mouth smirk.
- **Loop integrity** — scroll must be at canonical partial-roll with both seals visible at t=0 AND t=loop-end. The over-unfurl is mid-loop only.
- **Off-frame risk** — over-unfurled scroll bottom drops to about knee level (specific anchor) rather than open-ended. Static-camera lock prevents tilt-down misread.
- **Bureaucratic recovery** — small stiff shoulder shrug + composure smirk reassertion + small triumphant quill flourish.
