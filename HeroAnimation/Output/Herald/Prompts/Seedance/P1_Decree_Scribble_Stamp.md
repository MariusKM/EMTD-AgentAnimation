## Notes for this concept
- **Multi-beat sequence** (axis 5) — must enumerate the unfurl, the 3-4 scribbles, and the stamp finish as discrete timecoded beats so Seedance does not collapse them into one continuous arc (per Architect Stage 5 review lesson).
- **`unfurl` not `unroll`** — the entire concept is about extending and re-coiling the scroll. Every reference uses `unfurl` / `re-coil` / `coiled` per Architect P2 roll-family filter trap.
- **Open-mouth peak only mid-loop** — the warm closed-mouth smirk-grin is the FFLF; the wide beaming open-mouth grin is the climax beat at the stamp moment, then closes back to the smirk for the settle.
- **Re-noun the quill as a writing instrument** — in this concept the action is fundamentally writing/marking, not striking. Use `quill pen` and verbs like `marks`, `writes`, `notes`, `dots and dashes` rather than `stab`/`strike`.
- **Always-hold both props** — quill stays in left hand, scroll stays in right hand throughout. Lead Constraints with both imperatives.
- **Off-hand prop integrity** — the two red wax seals stay intact and at the same position on the scroll at t=0 and t=loop-end. The unfurl is partial only; the seals remain visible at the top of the parchment throughout.
- **Floating ink-glyph VFX** — black ink letterforms, drift upward and fade within ~0.3s of each scribble. Chroma-safe (no green elements).
