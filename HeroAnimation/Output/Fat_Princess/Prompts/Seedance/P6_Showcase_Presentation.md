## Notes for this concept

- **No kinetic force** — this is a pure theatrical showcase beat. Distinct from P1/P3 (impact-based) and P2 (comedic impact). Here the comedy is in the "ta-dah!" reveal of both props held aloft.
- **Cupcake integrity is absolute** — the cupcake is LIFTED and PRESENTED like a small trophy with the icing peak visible throughout. Any "waving" or "tossing" language is risky; use "lifted, held aloft, presenting" only.
- **Spoon figure-eight is exactly two loops** — not a spin chain. The chocolate-droplet ribbon follows the spoon path.
- **Mouth-closed FFLF** with a wide open-mouth ta-dah grin at the mid-loop peak only.
- **VFX**: pale-gold sparkle-halo around both props at the peak + chocolate droplet ribbon trail. No green.
- **Weight lifts onto toes at the peak** — gown lifts slightly, hat tassel sways up. Small theatrical "rising pride" gesture. Feet return to flat on settle.
