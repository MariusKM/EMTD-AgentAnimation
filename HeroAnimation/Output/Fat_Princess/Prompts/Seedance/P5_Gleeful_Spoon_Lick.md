## Notes for this concept

- **Direct realization of the client brief Hero Screen line** — "pulls out her spoon and gleefully licks it with a wide smile."
- **Cupcake stays locked in her right hand near her mouth and never makes contact with the spoon** — the lick involves only the spoon + her mouth. The cupcake is an immovable anchor prop for this concept.
- **Mouth does open for the lick peak** — this is one of the two power concepts where the mid-loop peak is explicitly open-mouthed (other is P6's ta-dah). Closed-mouth smirk is preserved for first/last frames only.
- **Pale-pink blush glow on cheeks at the peak is action-specific VFX** — acceptable per the lighting rule since it's a new "emotional-state" glow, not a re-description of the source-frame lighting.
- **No voice** — the lick is expressed with a soft savoring physical sound and a nose-hum, not a spoken "mmm."
