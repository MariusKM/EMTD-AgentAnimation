## Notes for this concept

- **Forward-projection axis** — this is the one power concept that PROJECTS her force TOWARD the viewer rather than vertically/horizontally. Strongest "hero screen" read because the chocolate splatter cone sprays toward the camera.
- **Risk of camera-zoom misread** — Seedance may interpret "thrusts forward toward the viewer" as a camera push-in. Mitigation: `static camera shot of...` prefix on the Subject line AND explicit "the camera does not move or zoom or push in" in Constraints.
- **Cupcake stays locked in her right hand near her mouth** — the thrust is single-handed with the spoon. No cupcake interaction.
- **Chocolate splatter cone is dark brown** — not green, not orange. Chocolate-themed VFX only.
- **Mouth-closed FFLF** — start/end closed-mouth smirk, open-mouth fierce battle grin lives in the held peak only.
