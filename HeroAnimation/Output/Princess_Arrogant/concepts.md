## Power Movement Concepts (revised 2026-05-06)

> Every concept upgraded to hero-tier weight + power. Slate spans 3 kinetic axes: forward thrust (P1, P2), multi-beat throw + return (P3, P4), diagonal cut (P5). Every power move uses the slow-heavy-wind-up → held-charged-apex → explosive-strike → held-follow-through → slow-regal-return pattern. VFX-heavy spectacle slot is P4 (slate-mandatory).

### P1 — Imperial Cross-Slash
- **Weapon action**: Slow heavy committed wind-up — both arms draw back wide and loaded with stored power, both fans angled outward with silver pre-strike glow gathering. Brief held charged apex with imperial chin-up. Then explosive simultaneous diagonal X-slash forward — anatomical-right fan cuts upper-left → lower-right, anatomical-left fan cuts lower-right → upper-left, fans cross in front of her chest at the impact apex. Held imperial follow-through pose with both fans extended forward and chin up. Slow regal return to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smug knowing smirk held throughout — she knew the cut would land. Imperial chin-up on the wind-up and again on the follow-through (chin holds high, not dismissive — confident). Small satisfied brow-arch on the recovery.
- **Timing feel**: Slow-fast-slow with a held-charged-apex beat and a held-follow-through beat. Strong slow-fast-slow contrast that reads as a hero with real power, not a quick elegant flick.
- **VFX element (multi-component)**: (a) silver pre-strike glow gathering on both fans during the wind-up; (b) brilliant silver crescent slash-trails along each fan's path with weighted substance; (c) bright silver impact flash at the cross-apex where the fans meet; (d) multiple expanding silver shockwave rings radiating outward from the cross-apex; (e) scattered silver-white energy fragments bursting outward from the impact. All silver VFX fully dissipates before loop end.
- **Secondary motion**: Auburn updo holds with wisps lifting strongly on the strike and settling on the return; puffed sleeves flutter strongly; gold scalloped overskirt panels and pendant tassels swing wide with the shoulder rotation; crimson underskirt billows with the body motion.
- **Reference**: Diana double-slash upgraded to General P6 v3 weight pattern — paired hero-tier strike with multi-component VFX.
- **Kinetic axis**: Forward thrust + paired diagonal cut
- **Generation risk**: MEDIUM — weight language is the new requirement; recommend running first.

### P2 — Imperial Fan-Point Decree *(NEW — replaces Disdainful Snap-Snap)*

Slow committed wind-up — anatomical-right (chest-fan) hand draws back loaded with stored power, anatomical-left arm sweeps wide as counterbalance. Brief held charged apex with bright silver pre-strike energy gathering on the chest-fan, body coiled imperial. Then **explosive forward thrust** — she drives the open chest-fan forward in a sharp imperious power-point/stab toward the viewer, the open fan presented broad-face forward (Architect P4 broad-face presentation pattern). Held imperial power-pose at full extension with chin up smug satisfied. Slow regal return to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: This is "you are dismissed, with prejudice." Smug knowing smirk held throughout, imperial chin-up at the apex of the strike (not haughty — *certain*), small satisfied brow-arch on the follow-through.
- **Timing feel**: Slow heavy wind-up with visible power-step forward → held charged apex → explosive forward thrust → held imperial pose for an extended beat → slow regal return.
- **VFX element (multi-component)**: (a) bright silver impact flash at the open fan tip at the moment of the thrust apex; (b) multiple expanding silver shockwave rings radiating outward forward from the fan-tip in concentric waves; (c) scattered silver-white energy fragments bursting outward in shrapnel-of-light pattern; (d) short radial cracks of silver energy branching outward through the air; (e) brief silver wind-burst around her stance from the forward-step force; (f) silver pre-strike energy gathering on the fan during the wind-up that reads as charged power. All silver VFX fully dissipates before loop end.
- **Held peak**: At maximum extension, she stands in the imperial decree pose — open fan extended forward broad-face, chin up, smirk smug — held for a clearly visible extended beat.
- **Secondary motion**: Auburn updo holds with wisps lifting on the thrust; puffed sleeve of the thrusting arm extends powerfully; gold scalloped overskirt panels and pendant tassels swing forward with the body weight transfer; crimson underskirt billows with the power-step.
- **Reference**: Architect P4 broad-face press (forward thrust with weight) + General P6 imperial bearing.
- **Kinetic axis**: Forward thrust (broad-face presentation)
- **Generation risk**: MEDIUM — the broad-face presentation Constraint is critical to keep the fan from morphing on the forward thrust.

### P3 — Imperial Boomerang Strike
- **Weapon action**: Slow heavy committed wind-up — anatomical-right arm draws back loaded with imperial power, the open chest-fan rotated to a horizontal launch position with silver pre-launch glow gathering. Brief held charged apex with body coiled. Then **explosive forward throw** — she releases the fan in a powerful boomerang strike, the fan spinning rapidly tip-over-tip and traveling forward into the foreground tracing a brilliant silver crescent slash-trail. The fan curves through a sharp impact arc at mid-frame with a multi-component silver shockwave burst on the imaginary impact point. The fan boomerangs back through a clean curving return arc and locks back into her anatomical-right hand with a sharp synchronized catch-snap and silver burst. Held imperial follow-through with chin up satisfied. Slow regal return to canonical. Anatomical-left (hip) fan stays absolutely canonical-still throughout — both hands committed to the strike, no parallel idle business.
- **Character delivery**: Smug knowing smirk held throughout — she launches the strike with confident power, her gaze stays forward and chin up imperial, she does not track the spinning fan with her eyes (the power read is that she trusts the strike, not the comedic dismissiveness of "I don't bother to look"). Small satisfied chin-up on the catch.
- **Timing feel**: Slow heavy wind-up → held charged apex → explosive throw → spinning flight with weighted spiral trajectory → impact-arc burst at mid-frame → clean boomerang return → sharp catch-snap → held imperial follow-through → slow regal return.
- **VFX element (multi-component)**: (a) silver pre-launch glow on the fan during the wind-up; (b) brilliant silver crescent slash-trail spiraling continuously with the spinning fan-rim along the throw-and-return arc; (c) bright silver impact flash and multi-ring silver shockwave burst at the fan's mid-frame impact arc; (d) scattered silver energy fragments bursting outward at the impact point; (e) silver sparkle burst at the catch-snap; (f) light silver wind-trail behind the fan in flight. All silver VFX fully dissipates before loop end.
- **Secondary motion**: Auburn updo holds with wisps lifting strongly on the throw and settling on the catch; puffed sleeve of the throwing arm extends with the throw and recovers with the catch; gold scalloped overskirt panels and pendant tassels swing forward with the body's power-step into the throw; crimson underskirt billows with the body weight transfer.
- **Reference**: Multi-beat throw + return upgraded to General P6 v3 weight + Wilhelm P3 multi-component impact VFX.
- **Kinetic axis**: Multi-beat throw + return
- **Generation risk**: HIGH — multi-beat sequence + impact VFX + boomerang return + audio-safety harden. Discrete timecoded beats required.

### P4 — Twin Boomerang Whirlwind ⭐ *VFX-heavy spectacle (slate-mandatory)*
- **Weapon action**: Slow heavy committed wind-up — both arms draw back wide and loaded with imperial power, both open fans angled outward with silver pre-launch glow gathering on both. Brief held charged apex with body fully coiled. Then **explosive synchronized launch** — both fans launch outward simultaneously, anatomical-right fan spiraling rightward and anatomical-left fan spiraling leftward, the two fans tracing mirror-image figure-eight paths around her stance with brilliant silver crescent trails. The fans intersect once at the figure-eight crossover point above her head with a multi-component silver impact burst, continue their orbits, and boomerang back into both hands simultaneously with a synchronized catch-snap and silver bursts. Held imperial power-pose at the eye of her storm with chin up smug. Slow regal return to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smug knowing smirk held throughout — she stands at the center of her own spectacle with absolute imperial composure. Chin up at the held peak, half-lidded eyes radiate certainty of her own power. Small satisfied brow-arch on the synchronized catch.
- **Timing feel**: Slow heavy wind-up → held charged apex → explosive synchronized launch → sustained spectacle whirlwind for ~2s → synchronized catch → held imperial power-pose → slow regal return. Strong slow-fast-sustained-slow contrast.
- **VFX element (multi-component, anti-tone-down)**: (a) silver pre-launch glow gathering on both fans during the wind-up; (b) brilliant silver crescent slash-trails persistent and bright following each fan's full figure-eight path; (c) multiple layered silver crescent flashes stacked at the figure-eight apexes; (d) scattered silver-white energy fragments bursting outward, 15+ visible particles; (e) a swirling silver wind-burst around her stance at ground level with multiple radiating wind-cracks of light branching outward; (f) bright silver impact burst at the figure-eight crossover where the fans pass each other above her head; (g) silver sparkle bursts at each catch on the synchronized return. All silver VFX fully dissipates before loop end.
- **Held peak**: At max-spectacle (~mid-loop), she stands chin-up at the eye of her own storm with both arms slightly outstretched and twin fans whirling at peak orbit, all VFX layers visible together — the held-peak the spectacle requires. She is absolutely composed inside the storm — that is the visual statement of her power.
- **Secondary motion**: Strong cape-like ruffle through the gold scalloped overskirt panels; crimson underskirt billows with the wind-burst; auburn updo and tiara stay locked (she does NOT come undone — her power is contained and controlled); puffed sleeves flutter strongly; pendant tassels swing wide; loose hair wisps drift in the vortex wind.
- **Reference**: Wilhelm P3 multi-component impact + Princess Sweet P6 thorn-bloom-class spectacle scale, applied to twin-fan boomerang.
- **Kinetic axis**: Multi-beat throw + return + hero-tier spectacle (highest production value)
- **Generation risk**: HIGH — all spectacle patterns required + audio-safety harden + fan silhouette preservation + synchronized catch + held peak. Recommend running AFTER P1 lands cleanly.

### P5 — Imperial Diagonal Power-Strike
- **Weapon action**: Slow heavy committed wind-up — anatomical-left arm draws the open hip-fan back wide and downward loaded with stored power, anatomical-right (chest-fan) hand pulls back to a guard position near her face. Brief held charged apex with silver pre-strike glow on the slashing fan, body coiled. Then **explosive single diagonal slash** — anatomical-left arm drives the open fan in a powerful upward diagonal cut from lower-anatomical-right to upper-anatomical-left, fast and committed with hip rotation behind it. Brilliant silver crescent slash-trail and multi-component impact burst at the apex. Held imperial follow-through pose with the slashing fan extended at the upper apex and chin up satisfied. Slow regal return to canonical, both fans returning to their canonical positions.
- **Character delivery**: Smug knowing smirk held throughout — single committed power-strike with imperial bearing. No parallel flutter, no idle business — both hands are committed to the strike (chest-fan in guard, hip-fan slashing). Chin up on the apex (she's certain the cut lands), satisfied brow-arch on the recovery.
- **Timing feel**: Slow heavy wind-up → held charged apex → explosive committed slash → held imperial follow-through → slow regal return.
- **VFX element (multi-component)**: (a) silver pre-strike glow gathering on the slashing fan during the wind-up; (b) brilliant silver crescent slash-trail along the diagonal cut path with weighted substance; (c) bright silver impact flash at the upper apex of the cut; (d) multiple expanding silver shockwave rings radiating outward from the apex; (e) scattered silver-white energy fragments bursting outward; (f) short radial cracks of silver energy along the slash path. All silver VFX fully dissipates before loop end.
- **Secondary motion**: Auburn updo holds with strong wisp-lift on the slash; puffed sleeve of the slashing arm extends powerfully; gold scalloped overskirt panels and pendant tassels swing strongly with the hip rotation; crimson underskirt billows with the body's power transfer.
- **Reference**: Wilhelm P2 horizontal sweep weight pattern → applied as upward diagonal cut with multi-component impact.
- **Kinetic axis**: Diagonal cut with imperial weight + spectacle
- **Generation risk**: MEDIUM — single-arm diagonal cut with held follow-through; weight language is the requirement.

## Idle Movement Concepts

### I1 — Breathing baseline *(mandatory)*
- **Action**: Continuous ambient breathing — slow calm chest rise/fall barely perceptible across the loop, no discrete visible breath events. Micro weight shift between feet.
- **Expression**: Cool half-lidded smirk held steady. Resting eye position (half-lidded looking slightly down at viewer per canonical pose). Occasional slow blink.
- **Secondary motion**: Auburn updo wisps drift almost imperceptibly with ambient breathing; earrings have ambient sway; pendant tassels micro-drift; both fans held perfectly still in canonical positions.
- **Duration feel**: 4s steady continuous-rhythm loop.
- *(Per Princess Sweet I1 lesson 2026-04-30: continuous ambient rhythm framing, full breath-ban in Constraints, no discrete breath events.)*

### I2 — Disdainful Face-Veil Flutter *(optional)*
- **Subtle action**: The anatomical-right (chest-height) fan flutters slowly in front of her face — a slow ambient flutter rhythm partially veiling the smirk. The flutter is gentle and refined, not vigorous. Chin slightly tilted up. Half-lidded eyes occasionally shift sideways as if dismissing someone off-screen.
- **Expression**: Smirk held, occasional smug brow-arch on the side-glance, slow blinks.
- **Breathing/weight**: Calm ambient breathing per I1; subtle weight shift onto the back leg as if leaning away from whoever she's dismissing.
- **Secondary motion**: Earrings swing gently with the chin-tilt; auburn updo holds; pendant tassels micro-drift; anatomical-left (hip-level) fan stays canonical-still throughout.
- **Duration feel**: 4s loop. Two slow flutter cycles + one side-glance.
- **Reference**: Spy-style narrative idle — small character business that builds personality without big motion.

### I3 — Slow Rhythmic Fan-Snap *(optional)*
- **Subtle action**: Both fans give a slow rhythmic snap-shut → hold one beat → snap-open in sync. Single cycle across the loop. More controlled and refined than the P2 power-move snap.
- **Expression**: Smirk held; eye-roll subtly on the snap-shut beat; smug satisfaction on the snap-open. Ambient calm between beats.
- **Breathing/weight**: Calm ambient breathing per I1; tiny weight settle on the snap-shut.
- **Secondary motion**: Earrings swing on each snap; auburn updo wisps lift slightly with each snap; pendant tassels swing in rhythm.
- **Duration feel**: 4s loop with one snap cycle (~1.5s of held-mid-snap, rest is canonical).
- **Reference**: Like a slowed-down version of the P2 attitude beat — adds ambient prop-business to the resting state.
