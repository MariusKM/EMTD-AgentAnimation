## Notes for this concept

- **Mandatory baseline idle** — strictly breathing-only, no character business.
- **Canonical pose preserved exactly** — cool half-lidded smirk, both fans open in their canonical positions, S-curve posture.
- **Continuous ambient breathing rhythm** (per Princess Sweet I1 lesson 2026-04-30) — no discrete visible breath events.
- **Avoid** the words `slow deep quiet breaths` / `slow deep breath` / `single deep breath` anywhere.
- **Constraints leads with the full breath-ban** (per Princess Sweet I1).
- **No I2 / I3 business** — no flutter, no snap, no chin-lift, no eye-roll, no side-glance. Those are variants.
- **Sound** uses only material/ambient sounds + soft natural ambient nose-breathing — no `nose-exhale` / `nose-snort` items (Architect 2026-04-28 quality lesson).
- **Always-hold both fans** at start of Constraints.
- **Natural relaxed idle, but skirt does not "breathe"** (AD feedback 2026-05-08, refined): v1 had the entire gold scalloped overskirt expanding and contracting as if it were the lungs. The fix is NOT to lock everything down — keep a natural relaxed idle with normal calm breathing and gentle secondary motion. The single specific ban is on the **skirt pulsing in time with the breath** (no rhythmic expand/contract, no balloon-like inflation). Small ambient drift on the skirt fabric from the body's micro weight shift is fine; what's forbidden is the skirt visibly inflating and deflating on each breath.
