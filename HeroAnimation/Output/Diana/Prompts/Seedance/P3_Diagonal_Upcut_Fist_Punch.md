## Notes for this concept
- **Diagonal upcut variant of P1** — distinct kinetic axis (diagonal vs. P1's horizontal) and distinct VFX silhouette (straight-line diagonal trail vs. P1's curved arc).
- **Wilder personality reading** — hot-headed passionate Wildlands rebel rather than the composed flourish of P1. Open-mouth grin at the peak (vs. P1's closed-mouth grin briefly).
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — sword in **left hand** does the upcut, **right hand** does the fist pump simultaneously.
- **Static camera prefix** — upward sword motion + fist-up is a high-vertical-motion beat that Seedance can misread as a camera tilt-up. Lead Subject with `static camera shot of...`.
- **Always-hold sword** + **second-sword-stays-sheathed** at start of Constraints.
- **Augmented VFX** — straight-line diagonal slash-trail + 10-12 star-burst sparkles at upcut peak + secondary fist-punch sparks + drifting white embers from the trail tail + post-slash blade aura-gleam. White palette throughout.
- **Vocal direction** — wilder more passionate wordless shout, held a beat longer than P1 ("Yeaah!" / "Hyaaah!").
- **Loop integrity** — start and end frames match canonical.
