# Diana — Animation Concepts

## Reference
- Source art: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Diana.png`
- Hero description: `Docs/Heroes/Diana.md`
- Personality keywords: scrappy, confident, resourceful, brave, adventurous, independent, passionate, hot-headed, natural leader
- Weapon (primary): Short sword in anatomical-left hand at shoulder height, blade angled up-back behind head, gold spherical pommel + crossguard
- Weapon (secondary): A **second matching short sword** sheathed at the right hip — opens the door to **dual-wield** power moves per Silviu notes ("Dual wielder, attacks with both swords, performs whirlwind attacks")
- Hand convention: sword in **left hand** (anatomical), hip-rest hand is **right hand** (anatomical). Viewer's right side of image = her anatomical left.
- FFLF expression: canonical PNG already has a closed-mouth confident smirk — neutral resting expression, suitable for FFLF as-is. **No mouth-closed variant needed.**
- Garment-noun caution: source's gray-brown shoulder fur is a **fur shoulder collar / fur stole around both shoulders** — never write "mantle" (over-reads to full cape silhouette in Seedance).

## Reference Animation
Diana is the **gold-standard reference** for slash-trail VFX in `Examples/diana-with-aac-audio.mov` — the existing 6s reference shows a horizontal flourish-slash with prominent white slash-trail arc, double-slash crossing pattern, ~50/50 power-vs-idle split. She is also the named reference for "agile slashes" feel in the StyleGuide. Concepts below treat that reference as the **canonical anchor** — P1 explicitly mirrors it as a fresh I2V take, and other concepts diversify around it.

## Cross-hero kinetic-shape audit (sword/melee heroes)
Existing locked sword-hero kinetic shapes: New King P1 salute, P2 horizontal slash, P3 overhead chop, P4 vertical flourish, P6 forward point | General P1 salute, P2 forward thrust, P3 horizontal cut, P5 diagonal cut, P6 vertical sword-plant, P7 volley command (multi-beat) | Wilhelm P1 stab, P2 sweep, P3 overhead slam, P4 plant, P5 aurora storm | Blacksmith P9 forge strikes, P10 side hammer sweep.

Diana's kinetic distinctiveness comes from **speed + agility + dual-wield** — the slash-trail intensity, fast tempo, and dual-blade option are her signature. Concepts below lean into agility (one snap-fast flourish vs. the heavier slow wind-ups of King/General/Wilhelm) and reserve a dedicated dual-wield concept that no other hero can do.

## Power Movement Concepts

### P1 — Flourish Slash & Fist Pump ⭐ *(canonical client-direction beat — horizontal arc + held verdict)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (left hand sword at shoulder, right hand on hip), brief crisp anticipation pull-back of the sword (~0.3s, blade arcs slightly further back behind the head). Then a **fast wide horizontal slash from her anatomical-right to anatomical-left** at chest height — the blade sweeps in front of the body in a single decisive arc, leaving a **bright white slash-trail VFX arc** that lingers ~1s before dissipating. As the slash completes, the **right hand pumps up into a confident fist-punch into the air** (anatomical right, viewer's left side of image) at face/shoulder height, held briefly. Sword recovery: blade swings back up to its canonical raised position behind the head, fist drops back to the hip. Settles to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → fierce focus on the slash → brief triumphant grin at the fist-pump peak → smirk again. Confident, agile, hot-headed leader energy.
- **Timing feel**: Fast and snappy. ~0.3s anticipation, ~0.4s slash, ~0.6s held fist-pump, ~0.5s recovery, ~0.2s settle. Quick attack speed per CSV.
- **VFX element**: Bright **white slash-trail arc** — the Diana signature. Sharp leading edge, soft fading tail, ~1s persistence. **Augmented VFX layers** (added 2026-05-06): (a) a small **bright white spark-burst** (5-7 distinct sparkle particles) at the slash midpoint as the blade reaches peak velocity; (b) a **soft white aura-gleam** that runs down the blade for ~0.3s post-slash as it returns to the canonical raised position; (c) at the fist-pump peak, a **small bright white star-flash** pops at the knuckles (single-frame bright pulse plus a faint expanding light ring, ~0.4s) — reads as the triumphant impact-of-victory beat. No second slash (kept simpler than the reference's double-slash so the held fist-pump verdict reads cleanly).
- **Secondary motion**: Red hair bounces and follows the head with slight delay; teal cape flares outward on the slash and settles back; fur shoulder collar has subtle jiggle on the slash impact frame.
- **Reference**: `Examples/diana-with-aac-audio.mov` — fresh I2V take of the canonical Diana reference, but tightened to a single slash + fist-pump rather than double-slash.
- **Vocal direction**: A short **sharp wordless battle-cry shout** ("Ha!" / "Hyah!") at the slash impact — short, brave, hot-headed. Pair with sword-cut whoosh + slash-trail shimmer + fist-impact-air whoosh.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: CSV Hero Screen: "quick, graceful flourish with her sword, finishing by punching her fist into the air with a confident and passionate expression." Direct match.
- **Kinetic axis**: Horizontal arc / sweep + held no-force verdict pose at the peak.

### P2 — Twin-Blade Cross Slash *(dual-wield — multi-beat sequence)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (~0.3s), her **anatomical right hand swiftly draws the second short sword from the right hip** in a quick fluid motion — second blade now held in the right hand at shoulder height (mirroring the canonical left-hand pose, briefly held in dual-wield ready stance, ~0.3s). Then **two crossing slashes in rapid succession**: first the **left-hand sword cuts diagonally from upper-left to lower-right** across the body (~0.4s) trailing a bright white slash-arc; immediately followed by the **right-hand sword cutting diagonally from upper-right to lower-left** in the opposing direction (~0.4s) trailing a second white slash-arc — the two trails form a **bright X-shaped crossing pattern** in front of her chest that holds for ~0.5s before fading. **Critical loop-closure beat**: the right hand returns the second sword smoothly to its **sheath at the right hip** (~0.5s) and the left-hand sword returns to its canonical raised position behind the head, right hand back to hip. Settles to canonical (FFLF integrity preserved — second sword visible sheathed at hip at start AND end).
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → fierce dual-wield warrior face on the draw → focused intensity on the X-slash → triumphant grin held during the X-trail peak → smirk again at re-sheath. Showcases her dual-wield identity per Silviu notes.
- **Timing feel**: Fast and aggressive. The draw must be snappy (whip-fast, not ceremonial), the slashes are crisp and quick, the re-sheath is smooth and fluid.
- **VFX element**: **Two bright white slash-trail arcs forming a clear X-shape** in front of her chest at the peak. Arcs hold the crossing pattern visible for ~0.5s before dissipating. **Augmented VFX layers** (added 2026-05-06): (a) a **bright white spark-burst** (8-10 distinct sparkle particles) explodes outward from the X-intersection point at the moment the second slash crosses the first — reads as the percussive crossing-impact; (b) a sharp **silver-white blade-gleam streak** runs along each blade as it's drawn from the hip and again as it returns to the sheath (steel-flash micro-VFX); (c) a **faint expanding white light-ring** (~0.6s lifespan) radiates outward from the X-center at the peak before the arcs themselves dissipate — the radial pulse anchors the "moment of crossing" as the readable beat.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair flares and bounces on each slash; cape flares outward on the body twist; both fur collar shoulders jiggle on the crossing slashes.
- **Reference**: New territory — no other hero has dual-wield. Slash-trail aesthetic anchored on Diana reference.
- **Vocal direction**: A **two-syllable wordless battle shout** with a sharp inhale on the draw and a punching exhale on the X-cross peak — `"Hah!-HAH!"` style. Pair with two distinct sword-draw schwing sounds, two whooshes for the slashes, and a clean steel-on-leather sheath sound for the closing.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: Silviu notes — "Dual wielder, attacks with both swords, performs whirlwind attacks." User explicitly approved exploring dual-wield in Stage 1.
- **Kinetic axis**: Multi-beat sequence (draw → X-cross → re-sheath) + diagonal-X cut shape.
- **Risk note**: Multi-beat off-screen-element coordination + held-prop integrity (the second sword must stay reliably in the right hand during the slashes and back in the sheath at end). Apply discrete timecoded segments in the Action body per the multi-beat-prompt rule. The "always-hold" Constraints clause needs to specify: `left sword in left hand always` AND `second sword starts and ends sheathed at right hip`.

### P3 — Diagonal Upcut Fist Punch *(diagonal cut — agility-leaning variant of P1)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (~0.3s), brief drop of the sword tip behind her right shoulder (anatomical) for a short anticipation. Then a **fast diagonal upward cut from lower-anatomical-right to upper-anatomical-left** — the blade sweeps in a sharp straight-line diagonal trail (rather than a curved arc) up across the body, ending with the sword raised vertically overhead with the blade pointing up-and-back behind the head. **As the upcut completes, the right fist punches up into the air** simultaneously (anatomical right hand, viewer's left side of image) at face/shoulder height, held briefly with a bright triumphant grin. Sword and fist both recover smoothly back to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → wild fierce expression during the upcut → eyes flash open with a triumphant open-mouth grin at the peak punch → smirk recovery. Hot-headed passionate energy — more wild than P1.
- **Timing feel**: Fast, almost reckless. Slightly punchier and more aggressive than P1.
- **VFX element**: A **bright white straight-line diagonal slash-trail** (rather than a curved arc — distinct silhouette from P1). Trail holds the diagonal line clearly for ~0.6s before fading. **Augmented VFX layers** (added 2026-05-06): (a) a **bright white star-burst flash** (10-12 distinct sparkle particles radiating outward) at the upcut peak where the blade reaches its highest point — reads as the apex moment; (b) a **secondary smaller spark-burst** (4-5 sparkles) at the fist-punch peak; (c) **tiny drifting white embers** (5-6 small particles) lazily float upward from the slash-trail tail for ~0.8s as the trail dissipates — the ember-residue carries the trail's energy lingering after the slash; (d) a **faint white aura-gleam** runs down the blade as it returns to canonical position.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair flares wildly with the upward motion; cape lifts on the upward sweep.
- **Reference**: Diana slash-trail aesthetic but applied to diagonal kinetic axis (vs. P1 horizontal arc).
- **Vocal direction**: A wilder more passionate wordless shout — `"Yeaah!"` or `"Hyaaah!"` style — held a beat longer than P1's bark. Pair with sword whoosh + slash-trail crackle + fist-impact-air whoosh.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: CSV Victory: "Raises her sword high with a passionate battle cry, pumping her fist in the air." This concept folds the victory-arc gesture into the hero-screen power move.
- **Kinetic axis**: Diagonal cut.

### P4 — Fencer's Forward Lunge *(forward thrust — finesse over force)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (~0.3s), brief small wind-up — sword pulls back behind the head a touch further while the right hip pulls back in a fencer's coil (~0.3s). Then a **single fast forward thrust-and-lunge** — left hand drives the sword tip forward toward the viewer at chest height with the arm fully extended, blade horizontal, while her body coils forward into a low fencer's lunge stance (anatomical right foot extends forward, anatomical left leg braces back). **Right hand** sweeps backward behind her hip in a balancing flourish (matches the forward extension). **A bright white linear slash-trail streaks straight forward along the blade path** — a sharp line of light following the tip. Held lunge pose for ~0.6s with a fierce smirking gaze locked forward at the viewer (challenging stare). Recovers smoothly back to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → focused fierce eyes on the lunge → held intimidating smirk at the peak → smirk recovery. Finesse, agility, more graceful than P1's wide slash. CSV Movement Style: "more finesse than brute strength."
- **Timing feel**: Fast and precise — like a fencing riposte. ~0.3s wind-up, ~0.3s lunge, ~0.6s held pose, ~0.4s recovery.
- **VFX element**: **A short bright white linear slash-trail streaking forward along the blade path** — distinct from P1's curved arc. Sharp leading edge at the tip, ~0.7s persistence. **Augmented VFX layers** (added 2026-05-06): (a) a **bright concentrated white tip-flash** at the moment the lunge fully extends — a single-frame point-of-light pulse at the blade tip plus a small expanding white light-ring (~0.5s) radiating outward from the tip — reads as the "point of contact" beat; (b) **3-4 small white sparkle particles** trailing back along the blade-path streak as the trail dissipates; (c) a **subtle white speed-streak** running along the length of the blade itself during the forward extension (a rim-of-light effect on the blade's silhouette, ~0.4s) that fades on the held pose.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair sweeps back with the forward lunge; cape billows backward dramatically. Strong forward weight shift.
- **Reference**: Slash-trail aesthetic but linear forward shape — closer to a fencer's straight thrust than the canonical Diana sweep.
- **Vocal direction**: A **sharp short wordless punching exhale** — `"Tah!"` / `"Hut!"` — clipped, fencing-precise, very different from the wider battle-cry of P1. Pair with a tight sword-air-whoosh.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: CSV Default Attack Style — "Fast attack speed, long melee range. Professional fighter — stabs and slashes with sword." This concept covers the **stab** half of that line (the slashing variants are P1, P2, P3).
- **Kinetic axis**: Forward thrust / press.
- **Risk note**: "Forward lean / lunge toward the viewer" wording — Seedance reads forward-lean phrasing as a camera zoom-in (Spy lesson). Lead the Subject line with `static camera shot of...` and express the lunge as a character-internal beat (`her body coils forward into a low fencer's lunge stance with her front foot extending forward`) rather than `leans toward the viewer`.

### P5 — Whirlwind Twin-Blade Spectacle *(slate-mandatory VFX-heavy — dual-wield spectacle finale)*
- **Origin**: Slate-mandatory VFX-heavy concept (per pipeline rule — every hero gets one big-scope power concept). For Diana, the spectacle reading is **the whirlwind attack made fully manifest** — Silviu notes call out "performs whirlwind attacks" as her gameplay flair. This concept brings that to the hero screen as the high-spectacle option.
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (~0.4s), her **anatomical right hand swiftly draws the second short sword from the right hip** (snappy steel-draw, ~0.3s). Both swords now held at shoulder height in dual-wield ready stance. She executes **one full controlled 360-degree pirouette in place** (~1.2s) — body rotates clockwise as seen from above, both swords sweep through wide horizontal arcs around her at chest height. **Critical**: she turns ONCE in place, ending facing forward exactly where she started; the camera does NOT orbit, only the character turns. As the pirouette unfolds, **both blades trail bright white slash-arcs that wrap around her body in a whirlwind cyclone pattern** — multiple overlapping arc-trails (8-10 distinct white arc-segments at varying radii) form a radial vortex of light around her at the peak. Tiny **white sparkle particles** scatter outward from the cyclone (10+ distinct sparks). After completing the rotation, she settles into a held two-blade pose (~0.6s) with both swords angled outward in a wide stance, fierce open-mouth grin, before returning to canonical: right-hand sword sheathes back at the hip, left-hand sword returns to the canonical raised position behind the head. The whirlwind VFX dissipates fully before loop end.
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → focused warrior face on the draw → wild fierce eyes during the spin → triumphant open-mouth grin at the held two-blade peak → smirk recovery. Spectacle-tier hero-of-the-rebellion energy.
- **Timing feel**: Fast and exhilarating. The pirouette must be ONE complete rotation, not chained spins. The held peak gives the spectacle its readable beat.
- **VFX element**: **Multi-arc whirlwind cyclone of white slash-trails** wrapping radially around the character at chest height — 8-10 distinct overlapping arc-segments forming a dense radial vortex at the peak. Plus 10+ small white sparkle particles scattering outward. **Augmented VFX layers** (added 2026-05-06): (a) a **vertical column of white energy** rises from her feet to slightly above her head during the rotation — a translucent radiant pillar that frames the cyclone (the "eye of the storm"); (b) a **bright white shockwave ring** expands outward at ground level from the moment of full rotation (~0.6s expanding-ring lifespan); (c) **tiny drifting white embers** (12-15 small particles) hang in the air around her at the held two-blade peak before dissipating outward; (d) a **silver-white blade-gleam** runs along both swords as they're drawn from the hip and again as they're held in the final peak pose. Full dissipation before loop closes.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair fans outward dramatically with the rotation; cape billows in a wide skirt-like flare around her body during the spin and settles back; fur shoulder collar jiggles vigorously through the rotation.
- **Reference**: New spectacle territory — no other hero has dual-wield whirlwind. Princess Sweet P6/P7/P9 spectacle-tier concepts as VFX-density anchor.
- **Vocal direction**: A **rising wordless battle-roar** that builds across the rotation — `"Yaaah-HAH!"` style — passionate hot-headed peak. Pair with two sword-draw schwings, the whirlwind whoosh of crossing blades, and a final fierce held breath at the peak.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: Silviu notes — "Dual wielder, attacks with both swords, performs whirlwind attacks." This is the gameplay-canonical Diana reading.
- **Kinetic axis**: Rotational + multi-beat sequence (draw → spin → held peak → re-sheath).
- **Risk note** *(highest first-pass generation risk in the slate)*: (a) the **`twirl`/`spin` body-rotation read** must be locked precisely — explicit `exactly one complete 360-degree pirouette in place, ends facing forward` + `camera does not orbit only the character turns in place` per the Fat Princess P4 lesson; (b) **distinctive sword silhouettes morphing during rotation** — apply (A)+(B)+(C) silhouette preservation per Architect P4 lesson; (c) **second-sword integrity** — must start and end sheathed at right hip; (d) **VFX color collision** — Diana's slash-trail VFX is **bright white**, no green/yellow-green hue, so green chroma key compatibility is fine (no blue-chroma variant needed). **Authoring rule**: run P1 (or P3) FIRST so the keyer + prompt craft are tuned to Diana's quirks, THEN attempt P5.

### P6 — Wildlands Phoenix Slash *(slate-mandatory VFX-heavy — vertical/forward overhand spectacle, golden-flame palette)*
- **Origin**: Second slate-mandatory VFX-heavy concept, deliberately diversified from P5: **P5 = rotational + white-cyclone palette** (combat-spectacle reading); **P6 = vertical/forward overhand + golden-orange flame palette** (rebel-leader iconography reading). Diana is the "Wildlands Rebel" rebel leader — the phoenix-flame imagery channels her fiery red hair, hot-headed passion, and rebellion identity. Spectacle-tier "hero card" reading for the slate.
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (~0.4s), her **anatomical left hand raises the sword overhead** in a slow theatrical arc, blade tip pointing skyward, while simultaneously her **anatomical right fist raises overhead** in a banner-raising rebel-rallying pose (both arms extended upward, body opening wide in a "rally" stance). At the peak (~1.0s), the **blade ignites with a burst of golden-orange flame** — fire wraps the entire sword from pommel to tip, bright golden core with orange flame-tongues licking outward and small embers shedding upward. Held flaming-blade peak (~0.6s) with a fierce open-mouth battle-roar expression and head tilted slightly back. Then she swings the flaming sword forward and down in a **single decisive overhand vertical chop** (~0.5s) — the blade arcs from overhead down to chest height in front of her, leaving a **massive vertical golden-flame slash-trail** behind it. As the chop completes, the flame-trail expands outward and **paints the silhouette of a phoenix in mid-air in golden-orange flame** — wings spread wide flanking her body, head pointing forward over her shoulder, talons extending downward — held visible for ~0.6s. Phoenix silhouette dissipates outward into 20+ floating golden embers that drift upward and fade. Sword and right hand smoothly recover to canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Smirk → fierce determined intensity on the rallying overhead raise → wild open-mouth battle-roar at the flaming-blade peak → triumphant fierce focus on the chop → satisfied smirk recovery as the embers drift. Spectacle-tier rebel-leader-of-the-Wildlands energy.
- **Timing feel**: Slower and more theatrical than P1-P4 — the build to the flaming-blade peak gives the spectacle space to read. Total ~3.5s power beat (vs. ~2s for P1-P4).
- **VFX element**: **(1) Golden-orange flame wrapping the entire sword** during the held peak — bright gold core with orange flame-tongues, small upward-shedding embers (10+ particles); **(2) Massive vertical golden-flame slash-trail** following the chop arc — dense saturated golden-orange flame, ~1.5x the width of a standard slash trail; **(3) Phoenix silhouette painted in golden-orange flame** — wings spread, head forward, talons extended, briefly held in mid-air around her body before dissipating; **(4) 20+ floating golden embers** drift upward and outward as the phoenix dissipates, fading before loop end. Color palette intentionally **golden-orange flame** (not white slash-trail) — distinct VFX identity from P1-P5 and from the chroma green.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair flares dramatically with the overhead raise (her red hair visually rhymes with the golden-orange flame); cape billows upward and outward through the rally pose, then sweeps forward with the chop. Fur shoulder collar jiggles on the chop impact frame.
- **Reference**: New spectacle territory. Princess Sweet P6 / Dragonwitch dragon-flame for VFX-density anchor; Wilhelm P5 Aurora for vertical-element-with-spectacle-overlay structural reference.
- **Vocal direction**: A **rising wordless rebel battle-roar** that builds across the rally → peak → chop arc — `"Yaaaah-HAAH!"` with the peak shout landing on the chop. Pair with golden-flame ignition crackle/whoosh on the blade ignition, deeper roaring flame whoosh on the chop, and a soft fading flame-ember sizzle as the phoenix dissipates.
- **Source-of-truth tie**: CSV Role & Traits — "Wildlands Rebel Diana. Rebel leader, sometimes ally of player. Passionate and hot-headed, courageous and natural leader." This is the gameplay-canonical rebel-leader reading.
- **Kinetic axis**: Vertical down + held theatrical no-force peak — distinct from P5's rotational axis.
- **Risk note** *(highest first-pass generation risk in the slate, alongside P5)*: (a) **VFX color collision check** — golden-orange flame is fine on green chroma key (no overlap with green hue); no blue-chroma variant needed. (b) **Phoenix silhouette painted in flame** is an ambitious VFX read — Seedance may collapse it into a generic flame burst. **Authoring rule for prompt**: describe the phoenix as `a clear golden-orange flame silhouette in the shape of a spread-winged phoenix with wings flanking her body head pointing forward and talons extended downward, the phoenix is painted in flame and clearly readable as a phoenix shape` — apply the same elaborate-detail pattern as Architect P1 (`elaborate ornate detail` + 4-5 specific concrete elements + comparison anchor + Constraints negation of the simple-shape fallback). (c) **Distinctive sword silhouette** — the burning blade is the focal element; apply (A)+(B)+(C) silhouette preservation per Architect P4 lesson. (d) **"Phoenix" + "flame" + "rebel battle-cry"** vocabulary cluster — preemptively check for safety-filter risk; nothing in the cluster maps to known triggers, but if a rejection appears, audit for: any negated violence words, `roll`-family words, dense weapon-action verbs around the chop. (e) **Mood-music inference risk** — golden-flame + phoenix + theatrical rally has spectacle/heroic mood inference potential (per Princess Sweet P1/P6 lesson). Preemptively apply the explicit `no music no orchestral no swells no melodic tones` ban + drop any `warm`/`serene` mood modifiers from Action. Vocal direction is a battle-roar (mechanical-sound territory), which helps.

> **Per-hero kinetic-shape diversification check**: P1 horizontal arc, P2 multi-beat dual-wield X-cross (diagonal-X), P3 diagonal upcut, P4 forward thrust, P5 rotational + multi-beat spectacle, P6 vertical down + held theatrical peak. **6 distinct axes — passes.**

> **VFX-heavy concept count**: P5 (rotational white-cyclone whirlwind) + P6 (vertical golden-flame phoenix). **Two slate-mandatory spectacles** giving the team / client two visually distinct hero-card readings — combat-flair vs. rebel-leader iconography.

## Idle Movement Concepts

### I1 — Breathing Baseline *(mandatory)*
- **Action**: Continuous ambient breathing — chest moves gently and almost imperceptibly throughout the loop in a continuous smooth rhythm without any visible discrete breath events. Tiny weight shift between feet.
- **Expression**: Resting confident smirk held throughout — no expression arc.
- **Secondary motion**: Subtle red hair drift; teal cape drifts almost imperceptibly with the ambient breathing; occasional natural blink.
- **Duration feel**: 4s steady loop.
- **Vocal direction**: Soft natural calm ambient nose-breathing at a slow steady relaxed rhythm. No discrete deep breaths.
- *(This slot is filled the same way for every hero. It is the fallback option the team can always pick.)*

### I2 — Sword Shoulder-Tap & Smirk *(optional expressive variant)*
- **Subtle action**: Mid-loop, she taps the **flat of the blade** lightly against her left shoulder (anatomical) twice in a relaxed rhythmic motion — the kind of thing a confident scrappy fighter does while waiting. Right hand stays casually on her hip throughout. After the second tap, the blade returns to its canonical raised position behind her head.
- **Expression**: Resting smirk → small one-eyebrow-raise on the first tap → confident smirk widening slightly on the second tap → smirk recovery. Sly self-assured energy.
- **Breathing/weight**: Calm ambient breathing throughout; small weight-shift onto her front foot during the taps.
- **Secondary motion**: Red hair shifts slightly with each tap; cape has subtle ambient drift.
- **Duration feel**: 4s loop. Two taps at ~1.0s and ~2.5s, recovery to canonical by ~3.5s.
- **Vocal direction**: A soft contented wordless `"hmm"` closed-mouth hum on the first tap; calm ambient nose-breathing throughout. No actual words.
- **Risk note**: Sword stays in left hand always. Shoulder-tap motion is small-amplitude — no body twist, no big arm movement. Blade contact with the shoulder must be light (not a heavy slap).

### I3 — Confident Hip-Shift Smirk *(optional expressive variant)*
- **Subtle action**: She **shifts her weight from one hip to the other** in a small relaxed adventurer's pose — anatomical right hip pops out (~1.5s), held briefly, then rolls back to anatomical left (~3s). Throughout, her right hand stays on her hip and her left hand keeps the sword at shoulder height. Small confident head-tilt arcs alongside the weight shift — head tilts slightly to her anatomical right when right hip pops, returns to neutral on the rebound.
- **Expression**: Resting smirk → small confident eyebrow-arch on the right-hip pop → playful smirk-widen → smirk recovery. Cocky scrappy adventurer reading.
- **Breathing/weight**: Calm ambient breathing throughout. Weight shift is the entire kinetic content — no fidgets.
- **Secondary motion**: Hair drifts gently with the head-tilt; cape sways subtly with the hip-shift.
- **Duration feel**: 4s loop with one hip-pop cycle.
- **Vocal direction**: Soft contented wordless `"hmph"` mini-chuckle at the smirk-widen peak; calm ambient nose-breathing throughout. No actual words.
- **Risk note**: Avoid `rolls`/`rolling` for the weight shift (Fat King text-filter trigger) — use `shifts` or `sways`. Keep hip-pop subtle, not exaggerated.

> **Idle slate composition**: I1 mandatory breathing baseline + I2 sword-tap (prop-aware variant) + I3 hip-shift (body-only variant). User can pick the safe baseline (I1), the prop-flavored option (I2), or the body-language option (I3).
