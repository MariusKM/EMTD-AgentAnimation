## Notes for this concept

- **Glasses settle done with the chin, not a hand** — both hands are committed to props (blade + scroll bundle), so a hand-to-glasses gesture would mutate FFLF. The chin lifts a quarter-inch in a refined head-tilt back, visually settling the spectacles on the bridge of the nose without any hand reach. Reads as "I am simply too refined to use my hands for this."
- **Camera-tilt misread guard** — chin lifts but the camera stays locked. Pin in Constraints.
- **Spectacles catch a clean gold reflection** at the peak — small VFX detail that anchors the "settle" read.
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — blade in right hand, scrolls under left arm. Both absolutely still — zero hand movement during the loop.
- **Closed-mouth FFLF** — smug closed-mouth smirk holds. Brow arches a hair on the peak.
