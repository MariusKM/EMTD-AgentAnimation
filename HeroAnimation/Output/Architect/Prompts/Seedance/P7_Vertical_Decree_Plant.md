## Notes for this concept

- **New kinetic axis for Architect** — vertical down/plant motion, distinctly different from the horizontal arc / rotational flourish / forward press patterns in P1, P4, P5. Adds visual variety to the locked set.
- **Broad-face presentation throughout** (P4 silhouette-morph rule) — even though the blade moves vertically, the broad face with the curved protractor arc stays turned toward the camera so the silhouette doesn't morph.
- **The plant is at chest/waist height in front of him, not on the ground** — describe the plant point as "at waist height in front of him" or "at an invisible point at waist height" so the model doesn't have him bend down or strike the floor.
- **Survey-stake VFX** — small warm ink-blue diamond-shaped mark with two concentric ripple-glow rings, pulses outward and fades. Small ornate detail consistent with the P1/P5 VFX intricacy pattern but appropriate scale for a single point-mark rather than a full diagram.
- **Purpose framing** (article B2) — `as a survey-stake decree gesture` and `as a finalizing measurement decree`.
- **Closed-mouth FFLF** — smug smirk holds throughout, chin-tilt and brow-arch carry the personality.
