## Notes for this concept

- **New kinetic axis for Architect** — diagonal upper-right to lower-left sweep, distinct from P1's horizontal arc, P4's forward press, P5's rotational flourish, P7's vertical plant. Adds a fourth axis to the locked set.
- **VFX is a STRAIGHT RULED LINE not an arc** — the key differentiator from P1. Pin this in Action and Constraints. The line traces the diagonal blade path with fine tick-mark notches at regular intervals along its length and a small drafting-style numerical-looking notation near one end.
- **Broad-face presentation throughout** (P4 silhouette-morph rule) — even during the fast diagonal cut, the broad face of the blade stays turned toward the camera so the silhouette doesn't morph into a generic sword shape.
- **Diagonal direction explicit** — `from upper-right of the frame to lower-left of the frame` — without this Seedance defaults to horizontal.
- **Purpose framing** (article B2) — `as a measured-dimension cut`, `as a precise dimension-marking gesture`.
- **Single committed cut, not a flourish** — this is one decisive diagonal sweep, not multiple swipes. Architect's refined-fencer timing applied at a steeper angle than P1's horizontal arc.
