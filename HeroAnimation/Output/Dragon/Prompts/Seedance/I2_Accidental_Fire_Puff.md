## Notes

- **Single beat per loop** — exactly ONE accidental fire puff beat (no chained-doubled puffs per the Moneybags I2 single-beat lesson).
- **Tiny short-lived chibi flame puff** — no larger than the snout, lasts a fraction of a second, dissipates immediately. NOT a flame stream, NOT a sustained breath, NOT a deliberate fire-breath weapon.
- **Mouth defaults closed in canonical smirk; opens briefly mid-loop ONLY for the puff beat and returns to closed by loop end** (FFLF integrity).
- **VFX scoping language** per the Dragon-doc visual-anchor lesson: anchor with `puff` (NOT `breath`), `tiny` / `small` / `short-lived`, and explicit negation of streams / sustained burns / deliberate fire-breath / war-flames. Even with scoped language, fire-VFX risk is elevated on first pass — expect to iterate.
- **Expression arc**: smirk → brief widened-eye surprise → sheepish-recovery smirk. The teenage "oh, oops" register.
- Audio: layered small puff sound at the moment of the puff beat over the ambient breathing baseline.
