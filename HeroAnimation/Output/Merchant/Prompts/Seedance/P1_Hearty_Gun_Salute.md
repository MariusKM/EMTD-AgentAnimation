## Notes for this concept

- **Literal client brief read** — "jolly wave and then proudly shakes his gun, as if showing off his wares or defense equipment."
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — right hand on forward foregrip (viewer-left of image) stays on the gun throughout; **left hand on rear stock (viewer-right of image) is the one that briefly releases for the wave**. Per CLAUDE.md Fat King 2026-04-16 lesson, name the always-held hand explicitly and lead Constraints with "blunderbuss in two hands always" + "right hand on the forward foregrip always."
- **Static-camera prefix** — the overhead lift + shake is high motion that Seedance may misread as a camera move; lock down up front.
- **Open-mouth peak mid-loop only** — the jolly open-mouth laugh appears at the gun-lift peak (peak expression beat), opens and closes back to the canonical closed-mouth contented smile by t=loop-end. FFLF rule per Fat Princess 2026-04-23.
- **Feather noun risk** — "short white feather" with explicit Constraint negating "long plume / tall feather banner" extension (mantle/veil over-read family).
- **Backpack contents must not morph** — bread loaf, ham hock, grapes, apples are stable items that should not change shape; Constraint locks this.
- **No green VFX** — the small dust-glints on the gun shake are warm gold/cream, not green or yellow-green.
