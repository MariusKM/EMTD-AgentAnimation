## Notes for this concept

- **CSV default attack** — "Medium to slow attack speed. Reload through muzzle, aim, shoot. Puff of smoke after shooting. Not a professional warrior." This concept skips the reload beat and focuses on the brace → fire → recoil ritual.
- **Comedic stocky-recoil follow-through** — the belly + basket bounce on recoil is the personality beat. He's a heavy man with a heavy gun.
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — both hands stay locked on the gun the entire loop. No hand release.
- **Static-camera prefix** — fast forward fire + recoil wobble is exactly the "high motion misread as camera move" risk.
- **Open-mouth peak at the moment of fire only** — laughing grin at the fire peak, closes back to canonical closed-mouth smile on settle.
- **VFX color discipline** — warm yellow-orange muzzle flash + thick **gray-white** smoke puff. NEVER green. Smoke billows forward toward viewer's left then drifts upward and fades. No green sparks, no green glow.
- **Smoke direction** — gun barrel angles upward toward the viewer's left, so the muzzle smoke and flash exit toward the upper-viewer-left of the frame. Smoke drifts up and fades.
- **No "rolls" / "rolling" body motion language** — use `rocks`, `wobbles`, `sways` for the recoil per the Fat King 2026-04-16 lesson.
