## Notes for this concept

- **Overall idle direction**: Spy is restless, wired, slightly unhinged in a funny way — this is the "deep in his own thoughts, chuckling at an insane idea" flavor.
- The **eye drift up and to the side** at the start is critical — it sells "he's plotting" before the chuckle. Without that setup beat the chuckle reads as random.
- The **"idea strikes" beat** (tiny eyebrow raise + eye widen) is the punchline; the chuckle is the payoff.
- Mouth stays closed throughout — the chuckle is silent and lives in the shoulders, nose, and eyes.
