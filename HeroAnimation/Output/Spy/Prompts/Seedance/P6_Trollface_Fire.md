## Notes for this concept

- Threads the **trollface-to-camera** client signature through a clean **crossbow fire**. The grin is the personality; the shot is the payoff.
- **Fourth-wall break is in the EYES, not a head whip** — avoid chained head snaps (Seedance morphs quiver bolts and phantom capes on Spy's rapid head motion, per CLAUDE.md Known Issues). Eyes drift up and lock on camera; head can tilt very slightly.
- Eyes stay locked on camera **through the shot** — unsettling and funny at once. The crossbow fires off-axis while he stares at the viewer.
- Bolt exits **off-frame right** (or off-frame opposite to the crossbow aim direction).
- Single crossbow raise + loose, not chained. Grin widens slowly (not snappy). Silent shoulder-shake chuckle as recovery.
- Crossbow stays in his hands throughout — don't let it float.
