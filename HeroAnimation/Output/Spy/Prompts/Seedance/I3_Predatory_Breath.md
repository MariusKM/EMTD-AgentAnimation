## Notes for this concept

- **Overall idle direction**: Spy is restless, wired, slightly unhinged in a funny way — this is the "fidgeting with his weapon" flavor.
- Replaces the earlier "predatory breath / finger drumming" concept — drumming fingers on the crossbow stock is too subtle/complex for I2V models to resolve reliably. A single clear **heft/bounce** of the crossbow is a much simpler and more readable beat.
- The heft should be one clean motion, not continuous fidgeting — up an inch or two, narrow-eyed approving glance, back down.
- Energized tight breathing throughout to keep the wired/restless feel consistent with I1 and I2.
