## Notes for this concept

- The **understated** weapon-driven concept in the Spy set — glance, commit, fire. Less overt than P6's trollface; more the "stealth assassin" archetype.
- **Single sideways eye-glance, eyes-only (minimal head movement)** — explicitly avoids the chained head-snap morph risk (quiver/cape warping, per CLAUDE.md Spy Known Issues).
- Eyes return forward, crossbow raises smoothly from low-ready, aims briefly, **looses bolt off-frame**.
- Recovery: sly satisfied grin held to camera. Crossbow dips back to low-ready.
- No double-take, no head whip, no chained snaps.
