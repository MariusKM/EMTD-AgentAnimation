## Notes for this concept

- The **showoff assassin** variant — he's SO confident he winks while firing. Fully on-brand jovial/comical.
- **Wink and loose are simultaneous** — the wink IS the trigger pull, comedic sync.
- Crossbow raises into a deliberate steady aim, holds briefly, then the wink-and-fire happen together.
- Recovery: both eyes open with a wide trollface grin to camera.
- **No chained head snaps** (CLAUDE.md Spy risk). Controlled single-axis motion throughout.
- Optional tiny sparkle on the wink itself (Supercell-style polish) — drop in iteration if it distracts from the face.
