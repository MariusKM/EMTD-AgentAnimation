## Notes for this concept

- The **alibi beats** (paranoid left/right head checks) give the trollface a reason to exist — he got "caught" scheming, which is why he breaks the fourth wall. Much stronger motivation than a cold theatrical grin.
- Head snaps must be sharp and distinct — left, hold, right, hold, center — so the paranoid checking reads clearly. If they smear together the alibi fails.
- The **conspiratorial lean-in toward the viewer** happens together with the slow head-rotation back to camera — this is the "come here, let me show you something" moment that sells the fourth-wall break. The lean must be visible but not exaggerated (a few inches of upper-body tilt, not a deep bow).
- Eyebrow raise + trollface spread + silent chuckle land after he's already leaned in — they're the payoff, the lean is the setup.
- He straightens back up at the end so the canonical pose (upright hunch) matches for a seamless loop.
- Mouth stays closed during the silent chuckle finale.
