## Notes for this concept

- **Pure gentle princess** — no tomboy beat in this idle, just romantic-hopeful character. The contrast against I3 (Tomboy Wink) is intentional: I2 is the "perfect princess" idle, I3 breaks the mask.
- **Closed-mouth FFLF** — held gentle smile widens softly during the inhale, returns to canonical resting smile. No open-mouth peak.
- **Light secondary VFX**: a small handful of red and pink rose petals stream away from the rose toward screen-right on a gentle breeze during the inhale (AD direction 2026-05-11 — v1 had petals dropping straight down which read too static; restore the v0 wind-blown rightward flow). Subtle, not a full bloom.
- **Petals MUST fully fade away before the loop ends** (AD feedback 2026-05-08): in v1 the petals lingered all the way to the loop close, breaking FFLF integrity. The petals should appear briefly at the inhale beat, drift down a short distance, fade out smoothly, and be **completely invisible** by the time the rose returns to chest height. The final ~1.5s of the loop must show no petals at all.
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — rose lifts to her nose with the right hand. No hand swap.
- **Always-hold rose** at start of Constraints.
- **No twirl/spin** language — same as P5/I4 — hand is stable on the rose stem during the lift.
