## Notes for this concept

- **Vertical-down axis with inverted tone** — graceful descent + rising VFX, distinct from any "smash" beat across the roster.
- **Full-body motion** — the curtsy is the only concept on the slate where her whole body moves significantly. Watch for FFLF dress silhouette mismatch if the dip is too deep — keep the dip controlled so the wide ball-gown silhouette stays read.
- **Vines rise from below** — vine spiral grows around her body from the floor upward (around the dress, NOT through it) and peaks at her shoulders dissolving into petal motes.
- **Chroma rule** as P1 — olive yellow-green vines, no bright/lime/spring green.
- **Tomboy reveal beat** as P1 — brief mischievous half-grin just before the head-dip resolves.
- **Static-camera prefix** — the rising vine spiral is a vertical VFX growth that Seedance may misread as a camera tilt-up.
- **Always-hold rose** at start of Constraints. Left hand briefly touches the dress at the curtsy bottom, returns to relaxed-at-side on the rise.
