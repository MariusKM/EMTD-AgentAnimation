## Notes for this concept

- **Origin**: Art team feedback 2026-04-28 — slate needs powerful hero concepts. P8 is the vertical-axis sibling of P7 (lateral) and P9 (forward).
- **Two-stage structure**: gather-up (vines erupt into vertical pillar above her head, "queen of thorns" hold) → release-out (arms-down sweep triggers radial vine shockwave around her at body level).
- **Audio-safety preemptive (v1 from prompt start, per A6 lessons learned on P1 v1→v2 and P6 v1→v5)**:
  - Action body uses NO `warm`, `soft`, `serene`, `peaceful`, `calmly`, `widening calmly`, `gentle breeze` mood modifiers (per A6 family 2 — these shape audio-model mood inference toward orchestral content).
  - Sound line uses ONLY mechanical/material sounds (cloth rustle, vine creak, leaf rustle, sharp whoosh on the release, papery petal rustle). NO `chime`, `bell`, `tone`, `melodic`, `harmonic` (per A6 family 2).
  - Constraints leads with explicit positive `no music no orchestral no swells in audio only material and mechanical sounds` (per A6 family 2 + A3).
  - NO `spell`, `cast`, `incantation`, `summon`, `magic` nouns in Action body (per A6 family 3). Describe gesture mechanically: `she raises both arms overhead and brings them down sharply`.
  - Vines pass through midair around her body without contacting her dress or skin (per A6 family 1).
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — rose stays in **right hand** throughout (anatomical, viewer's right side of image since back-3/4). Both arms raise overhead together then come back down. No hand swap.
- **Body discipline** — body stays in canonical back-3/4 pose throughout. Both arms move; no body rotation, pirouette, or facing change.
- **Static-camera prefix** — vertical pillar growth + radial outward expansion is exactly the type of high-motion VFX Seedance reads as a camera tilt-up or pull-back. Lock down up front.
- **Loop integrity** — original red rose stays in right hand throughout; both arms return to canonical positions before loop end; all vines and petal motes fully exit/dissolve before loop end so FFLF first/last frame match.
