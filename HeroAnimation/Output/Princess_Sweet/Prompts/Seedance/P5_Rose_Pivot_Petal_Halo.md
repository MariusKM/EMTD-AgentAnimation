## Notes for this concept

- **⚠ TWIRL/SPIN VOCABULARY BAN** (per Fat Princess P4 lesson 2026-04-23) — Seedance reads `twirl` and `spin` as **full body rotation** even when the prompt says "rose only" or "in her hand." This concept must avoid both words entirely. Use phrasing like `the rose head pivots`, `the rose stem rotates between her fingers`, `the rose head turns slowly in her hand`. The Constraints line must explicitly state `she does not turn or spin her body the rotation is only the rose head pivoting in her fingers`.
- **Hand-only "rotational" beat** — differentiates from Architect P5 (full-body geometric flourish) and New King P4 (full-arm sword wrist flourish) by staying entirely at the rose-and-wrist scale.
- **Chroma rule** as P1 — olive yellow-green vines, no bright/lime/spring green.
- **Tomboy reveal beat** as P1 — brief mischievous half-grin on the settle.
- **Static-camera prefix** — the petal halo orbiting her wrist could be misread as a camera orbit. Lock down up front.
- **Always-hold rose** at start of Constraints. The rose stem rotates in her fingers but the rose itself never leaves her right hand.
