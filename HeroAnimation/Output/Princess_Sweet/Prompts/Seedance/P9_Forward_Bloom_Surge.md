## Notes for this concept

- **Origin**: Art team feedback 2026-04-28 — slate needs powerful hero concepts. P9 is the forward-axis sibling of P7 (lateral) and P8 (vertical).
- **Forward-projection at camera** with foreshortening — vines visibly larger at the camera-end of the wave for cinematic depth (Wilhelm-axe-slam technique applied to magical projection instead of a blade).
- **Audio-safety preemptive (v1 from prompt start, per A6 lessons)**:
  - Action body uses NO `warm`, `soft`, `serene`, `peaceful`, `calmly` mood modifiers (per A6 family 2).
  - Sound line uses ONLY mechanical/material sounds. NO `chime`, `bell`, `tone`, `melodic`, `harmonic` (per A6 family 2).
  - Constraints leads with explicit positive `no music no orchestral no swells in audio only material and mechanical sounds` (per A6 family 2 + A3).
  - NO `spell`, `cast`, `incantation`, `blast`, `magic` nouns in Action body (per A6 family 3). Describe gesture mechanically: `she thrusts the rose forward toward the viewer while her left hand pushes palm-forward toward the viewer`.
- **CRITICAL camera-misread risk** (per Spy lesson): forward thrust + lean toward viewer reads as a camera push-in to Seedance, NOT a character gesture. Mitigations applied:
  - Lead Subject with `static camera shot of...`
  - Avoid `leans forward toward the viewer` phrasing entirely; use `she thrusts the rose forward at chest height with her arm extending forward` (character-internal arm motion, not body lean)
  - Explicit Constraint: `the forward thrust is a character-internal arm motion the camera does not push in or zoom toward the action the camera stays locked`
  - **Foreshortening described as a property of the VFX (vines visibly larger at the camera-end of the wave), NOT a camera property.**
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — rose stays in **right hand** throughout (anatomical, viewer's right). Both hands extend forward simultaneously for the two-handed cast; both hands return to canonical positions before loop end.
- **Body discipline** — body stays in canonical back-3/4 pose throughout. Arms extend forward and return; NO body lean, NO rotation, NO facing change.
- **Loop integrity** — original red rose stays in right hand throughout; both arms return to canonical; all vines and petal motes fully exit/dissipate before loop end.
