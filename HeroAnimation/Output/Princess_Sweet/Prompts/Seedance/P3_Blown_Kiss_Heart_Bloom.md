## Notes for this concept

- **Strongest comedy beat on the slate** — the heart bloom is overtly Supercell-jovial. Per project tone direction (2026-04-16) — "I am the perfect princess… and I just made a heart of thorns."
- **Two-stage VFX**: (1) vines form a heart-shape ring outline in front of her, (2) red rose petals fill the interior briefly, then the whole heart fades into petal drift.
- **Mouth-open risk**: the kiss involves a brief light pucker. Keep the lip motion small and resolve back to the closed-mouth smile for FFLF integrity. Not a wide kiss, not a smacking gesture — just a soft brush of the rose to her lips, a small forward kiss exhale, and resolve.
- **Chroma rule** as P1 — olive yellow-green vines, no bright/lime/spring green.
- **Tomboy reveal beat** as P1 — brief mischievous half-grin on the settle.
- **Static-camera prefix** — heart bloom expanding outward in front of her could be misread as a camera push-in. Lock down up front.
- **Always-hold rose** at start of Constraints. The rose briefly comes up to her lips then returns to chest height — never leaves her right hand.
