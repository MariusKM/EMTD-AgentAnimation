Subject:     stylized 3D game character, bearded king in gold crown, olive green tunic, maroon cape, holding silver sword
Action:      releases left hand from sword grip, rotates sword in a smooth one-handed vertical flourish with the blade catching warm light, completes a second wider flourish, then left hand returns to the grip, settles back to the original two-handed diagonal chest grip with a warm knowing smile
Camera:      locked, full body, centered, static
Style:       Supercell mobile game art, saturated hand-painted colors
Sound:       sword whoosh on each flourish, soft metallic ring, no VO
Constraints: no camera movement, no background/environment, no talking/lipsync, seamless loop start=end pose, 6s

- Cape drifts gently from the subtle body weight shifts
- Crown catches light during the motion
