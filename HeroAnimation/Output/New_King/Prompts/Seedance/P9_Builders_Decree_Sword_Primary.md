## Notes for this concept

- **Weapon-primary rework of old P8**. The sword stays held and central — the "Builder" identity reads via a brief free-hand addressing gesture layered on top, not via planting the sword in the ground.
- Opening beat: sword lifted smoothly from the two-handed diagonal chest grip into a **forward decree pose** (blade horizontal, extended forward, both hands on grip).
- Middle beat: **left hand briefly releases** and gestures outward to the side in a "hear ye" addressing motion while the right hand holds the sword steady forward. Left returns to grip.
- Recovery: sword rotates back into the canonical diagonal chest grip.
- Warm gold gleam travels along the blade on the lift. Emerald gems on the crossguard pulse softly at the decree peak. **No dust/plant effect** — the blade is never grounded.
- Cape drifts forward with the lift, settles. Scroll pouch sways with weight shift.
- Do NOT write "leans forward toward viewer" — express as "he shifts his weight forward into the decree" or keep it character-internal.
