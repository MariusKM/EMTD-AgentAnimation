# P1: The Royal Salute — Seedance 2.0 Prompt

> Hero: New King | Type: Power Movement | Duration: 6s | Model: Seedance 2.0
> Source image: `Source/Heroes Stylized/New_King.png`
> Concept: [concepts.md](../../concepts.md) — P1

## Prompt

Subject:     stylized 3D game character, bearded king in gold crown, olive green tunic, maroon cape, holding silver sword
Action:      lifts sword upward from two-handed chest grip in a smooth arc, blade rises to a perfectly vertical salute at face level — arm extended, blade straight and pointing directly upward, crossguard horizontal — holds briefly with proud expression as a bright VFX-style star gleam sparks off the tip of the blade, then lowers sword back down in a controlled arc, both hands return to the original diagonal chest grip, settles back to starting pose with a warm gentle smile
Camera:      locked, full body, centered, static
Style:       Supercell mobile game art, saturated hand-painted colors
Sound:       metallic sword ring on lift, soft fabric rustle, no VO
Constraints: no camera movement, no background/environment, no talking/lipsync, seamless loop start=end pose, 6s

## Negative Prompt

walk cycle, run animation, jumping, falling, camera movement, camera shake, zoom, pan, environment, background scenery, talking, lipsync, voice, realistic proportions, photorealistic, static image, freeze frame, flat lighting

## Notes

- Blade must be **perfectly vertical** at the peak — straight up, not canted or diagonal — with the crossguard horizontal, forming a clean cross silhouette
- Golden gleam travels along the blade as it rises
- At the salute peak, a **bright VFX-style star gleam** (4-point sparkle / anime-style shine) bursts briefly off the blade tip, then fades before the return arc begins
- Emerald gems on crown and crossguard also flash/catch light at the salute peak
- Cape lifts gently during upward motion, settles naturally on return
- Scroll pouch at hip sways from the body movement
- Expression arc: steady confidence → proud resolve at peak → warm smile on return
