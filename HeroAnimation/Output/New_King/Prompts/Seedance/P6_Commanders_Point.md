# P6: The Commander's Point — Seedance 2.0 Prompt

> Hero: New King | Type: Power Movement | Duration: 6s | Model: Seedance 2.0
> Source image: `Source/Heroes Stylized/New_King.png`
> Concept: [concepts.md](../../concepts.md) — P6

## Prompt

Subject:     stylized 3D game character, bearded king in gold crown, olive green tunic, maroon cape, holding silver sword with gold-and-emerald crossguard
Action:      releases left hand from the sword grip and extends the right arm forward toward the viewer, sword rotating to point tip-first directly at camera — arm nearly fully extended, blade perfectly horizontal, tip aimed forward — subtle forward lean into the point, holds the commanding point with a fierce determined gaze as a bright VFX-style star gleam sparks off the blade tip, then retracts the arm smoothly, left hand returns to the hilt, settles back into the original two-handed diagonal chest grip with steady confidence
Camera:      locked, full body, centered, static
Style:       Supercell mobile game art, saturated hand-painted colors
Sound:       low metallic sword ring on extension, soft fabric whoosh of cape, subtle chime on the tip gleam, no VO
Constraints: no camera movement, no background/environment, no talking/lipsync, seamless loop start=end pose, 6s

## Negative Prompt

walk cycle, run animation, jumping, falling, camera movement, camera shake, zoom, pan, environment, background scenery, talking, lipsync, voice, realistic proportions, photorealistic, static image, freeze frame, flat lighting, blade curving, blade bending, wobbly blade

## Notes

- Blade must be **perfectly straight and horizontal** at the point peak — tip aimed directly at the viewer, crossguard vertical, clean forward silhouette
- Arm nearly fully extended — not bent, not tentative — confident committed forward reach
- At the point peak, a **bright VFX-style star gleam** (4-point sparkle / anime-style shine) flares off the blade tip, then fades before the retract begins
- Golden shimmer travels along the blade as it extends forward
- Emerald gems on the crossguard flash/catch light at the peak
- Cape swings back slightly as the torso leans into the point, settles on return
- Scroll pouch sways from the weight shift; crown catches light
- Expression arc: focused resolve → commanding authority at the point → steady confidence → calm return
- Timing: 1s extend → 1.5s point hold (with tip gleam) → 1.5s retract and re-grip → 2s settling/breathing
