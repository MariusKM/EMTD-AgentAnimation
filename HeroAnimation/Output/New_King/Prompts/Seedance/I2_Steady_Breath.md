Subject:     stylized 3D game character, bearded king in gold crown, olive green tunic, maroon cape, holding silver sword in two-handed grip at chest height
Action:      very slight rhythmic grip adjustment on sword, minimal head movement, slow blink, remains in the same standing pose throughout, natural subtle breathing
Camera:      locked, full body, centered, static
Style:       Supercell mobile game art, saturated hand-painted colors
Sound:       ambient silence, soft breathing, no VO
Constraints: no camera movement, no background/environment, no talking/lipsync, no sudden movements, seamless loop, start=end pose, 4s

## Notes

- Quiet confidence, gentle closed-mouth smile, eyes forward
- Breathing is natural secondary motion only — not the focus of the animation
- The most minimal of the three idle concepts — barely perceptible motion
- Cape gentle drift from ambient breeze
- Blade catches light subtly with the micro grip adjustment
