## Notes for this concept

- **Mandatory baseline idle** — strictly breathing-only. No character business. Safe fallback the team can always pick.
- **Continuous ambient breathing pattern** (Princess Sweet I1 / Dragonwitch I1 lesson) — frame as continuous ambient rhythm with no discrete visible breath events, NOT discrete cycles. Breath is "barely perceptible" / "ambient" not "deep" / "sharp" / "quick" / "short". Drop "no voice", use "no actual words no actual speech no music".
- **Concrete audible Sound** (Merchant I1 lesson) — describe SFX with concrete audible language (`a soft cloth shift`, `a soft metallic clink`, `a faint metallic shimmer`) rather than `faint`/`almost imperceptible`/`ambience`. Add explicit positive Constraint that the audio is audible and not silent.
- **NO nose-breath items in Sound** (Architect 2026-04-28 lesson) — drop all `nose-inhale`/`nose-exhale`/`nose-snort`. Use mechanical/material sounds incidental to the body motion: cape cloth shift, plate armor clink, leather creak, plume cloth shift. Soft natural calm ambient nose-breathing is fine as a single broad item but no discrete nose-breath events at specific beats.
- **Held canonical pose throughout** — both hands on pommel, right on top, left below. Stern stoic expression unchanged. One slow blink. Cape and plume drift almost imperceptibly with the breathing.
- **No I2/I3 business** — no eye-scan, no pommel-resettle. Those are variants.
