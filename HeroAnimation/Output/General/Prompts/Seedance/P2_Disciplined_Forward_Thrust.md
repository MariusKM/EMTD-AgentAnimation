## Notes for this concept

- **Single deliberate strike** — no chamber wind-up, no double-tap. Tighter and heavier than Diana's flick, lighter than Wilhelm's slam. CSV: "slower but more powerful than Diana."
- **Static-camera prefix mandatory** — forward thrusts are exactly the case Seedance reads as a camera push-in. Lock down up front. Do not write "leans forward" — express forward action as a half-step body motion.
- **Two-handed grip throughout** — both hands grip the hilt during the thrust, right on the grip and left bracing low. No transition to single-hand.
- **Tight straight-line VFX** — clean white blade trail along the thrust path, NOT an arc. Distinct from Diana's wide swoosh.
- **No body lean toward camera** — character takes a small step forward in place. The thrust is a character-internal arm extension.
- **Filter-safe verbs** — `drives forward`, `extends`, `pulls back`. Avoid `stab`, `pierces`, `impales`. Target is empty air, not a body.
