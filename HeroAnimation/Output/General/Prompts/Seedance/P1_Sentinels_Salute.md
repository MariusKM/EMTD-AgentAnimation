## Notes for this concept

- **No-force theatrical pose** — heels click together, sword raises blade-up to a held salute, lowers back to sentinel grip. Personality through stillness, not motion arc.
- **Anatomical hand discipline** — right hand on top of pommel (dominant) lifts the sword off into the salute; left hand drops to the side at attention during the salute, returns to the pommel on the lower.
- **Static-camera prefix** lead — even though motion is contained, the sword vertical lift is exactly the kind of beat Seedance sometimes reads as a tilt. Locked up front.
- **VFX is restrained** — single specular highlight traveling up the polished blade as it raises. No magic, no shockwave. This concept's drama is the discipline.
- **Stoic-only vocal direction** — no smirks widening, no chuckles. Single quiet stoic exhale equivalent expressed as plate-armor settle sounds + cape cloth shift. NO nose-breath items per CLAUDE.md (snivelling risk).
