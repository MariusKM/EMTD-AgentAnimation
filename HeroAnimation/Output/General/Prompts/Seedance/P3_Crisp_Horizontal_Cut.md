## Notes for this concept

- **Single disciplined slash, no double-tap** — differentiates from Diana who does two slashes with crossing trails. General does one decisive horizontal cut, then back to sentinel. No flourish.
- **Static-camera prefix mandatory** — fast horizontal arc motion is the primary camera-pan misread case.

- **Trail is single tight white horizontal arc** — sharper, tighter, more disciplined than Diana's wide swoosh. No second slash.
- **Mid-weight feel** — heavier than Diana's snap, lighter than Wilhelm's slam. Crisp military timing.
- **Vocal direction** — single quiet stoic exhale through the body, expressed as plate-armor settle + cape cloth rustle (no nose-breath items per CLAUDE.md).
