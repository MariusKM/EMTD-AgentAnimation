# Animation Concepts — Fat King

> Stage 2 working doc. Concepts are model-agnostic — prompt translation happens in Stage 3.

## Character Context
- Source art: `Source/Heroes Stylized/Fat_king.png`
- Hero description: `Docs/Heroes/Fat_King.md`
- Personality keywords: jolly, rotund, generous, elegant, bored (seeking entertainment), indulgent, theatrical, warm, good-natured, self-satisfied (amiably so)
- Weapon/signature: croissant pole-arm (golden scepter with a ribbed crescent croissant head) — held upright in right hand
- Tone direction: jovial / slightly comical (client, 2026-04-16) — Fat King is a "lean hard into it" character
- Animation focus: **the belly is the comedic centerpiece**; motion style is heavy, slow, rolling/wobbling
- Canonical pose for start/end frame: three-quarter view, belly pushed proudly forward, left hand on hip, right hand holding golden croissant scepter upright, ermine scarf draped across shoulders, warm jolly smile

## Power Movement Concepts

### P1 — Hearty Belly-Laugh  ⭐ (client brief signature)
- **Action**: From canonical pose, scepter plants with a satisfying thump. Chin rises, mouth opens wide in a belly-laugh. Free hand leaves hip and joins the scepter hand — **both hands now slap-pat the belly** rhythmically (three hearty pats) as he wobble-laughs. Head rocks back, belly visibly jiggles, tears-of-laughter squint. Pats slow and trail off into a warm sigh, hand returns to hip, scepter upright, settled back to canonical with a satisfied afterglow smile.
- **Emotional arc**: Warm smile → building laugh → full belly-guffaw → sigh of contentment → canonical
- **Timing feel**: Slow build, middle-section rhythmic belly-pats, slow settle. Heavy wobbling motion throughout.
- **VFX element**: Minimal — small pale-gold sparkles puff around his belly on the pat-peak beats (delight clouds); optional laugh-tear glints at eye corners
- **Secondary motion**: Belly wobbles HARD, ermine scarf bounces on shoulders, wig curls jiggle with each head-rock, coat-tails sway
- **Reference**: Wilhelm (weight/timing) but warm and jolly instead of menacing. Direct realization of client brief Hero Screen ("patting belly, wobbling laugh").
- **Duration**: 6s

### P2 — Croissant Scepter Proclamation
- **Action**: Scepter lifts and plants down firmly with a theatrical thump. He draws himself up to his full (round) height with a puffed chest, chin tilts up aristocratically, free hand leaves hip and sweeps outward in a grand "hear ye, hear ye" gesture. Mouth opens for a bold declaration — holds the regal pose a beat — then breaks character, flashes a **warm conspiratorial wink to camera** with a little belly-bounce chuckle. Free hand returns to hip, scepter upright, canonical.
- **Emotional arc**: Proud royal bearing → grand declaration pose → wink/warm break → canonical
- **Timing feel**: Slow rising build (pompous), held beat, snap-wink release. Anticipation-to-punchline ratio gives it the comedy.
- **VFX element**: Small gold sparkle-ring expanding outward from the croissant scepter head when it plants (a "tah-dah" flourish); tiny gold glimmer on the wink
- **Secondary motion**: Ermine mantle lifts slightly as he draws up, wig curls settle as he peaks, belly jostles on the chuckle, coat-tails sway on the sweep
- **Reference**: Moneybags (theatrical showmanship) + General (proud bearing) with a jolly wink payoff
- **Duration**: 6s

### P3 — Royal Coin Shower  ⭐ (generosity signature)
- **Action**: Free hand reaches into the coat, pulls out a bulging red velvet coin purse. Holds it up, gives it a playful shake (jingle-shake), shoots a **generous twinkling smile at camera** — then **tosses it overhead**. Purse bursts mid-air and **rains gold coins down** around him. He spreads his free hand wide, throws his head back in a delighted "HA!" belly-laugh as coins shower past his shoulders and bounce off his belly. Coins settle out of frame. Laugh trails off, hand returns to hip, canonical.
- **Emotional arc**: Warm smirk → generous reveal → coin-toss release → delight/belly-laugh → satisfied settle
- **Timing feel**: Deliberate build on the purse reveal, snap on the toss, sustained coin shower, slow settle
- **VFX element**: **Gold coin particle shower** (Moneybags-style individually animated coins with parabolic arcs and tumbling), bright warm gold glint on the purse-burst, tiny sparkle dust following the coins
- **Secondary motion**: Belly bounces on laugh, ermine scarf shifts on the toss, wig curls bob on the head-throw-back, coins bounce off belly adding micro-bounces
- **Reference**: Moneybags (coin VFX inheritance) — but the character read is **Fat King gives coins away joyfully** while Moneybags hoards them. Same VFX family, opposite emotional register.
- **Duration**: 7s

### P4 — Bored Royal Spots Entertainment  ⭐ (captures "always bored, looking for entertainment")
- **Action**:
  1. Opens with a bored **yawn** — chin tilts up, free hand politely covers mouth, belly and wig wobble with the yawn
  2. Hand drops. Eyes wander listlessly left, then right (drooped eyelids, half-lidded bored look)
  3. **Suddenly spots something off-camera**. Eyebrows shoot up, bored face transforms instantly into a **huge delighted grin**, eyes sparkle with glee
  4. He **claps hands together** in front of his chest — belly bounces sharply on the clap — and lets out a hearty "Ah, splendid!" belly-laugh, rocking forward
  5. Settles back into canonical with a lingering pleased smile and a small "oh my" shake of the head
- **Emotional arc**: Bored sigh → listless scanning → delighted recognition → joyful clap-laugh → warm contentment
- **Timing feel**: Slow drowsy opener, snap transformation on the spot-beat, explosive clap-laugh, slow settle. Huge emotional contrast is the comedy.
- **VFX element**: Small gold sparkle-pop above his head on the "lightbulb" spot-beat (delight cue); belly-bounce dust-puffs on the clap
- **Secondary motion**: Belly wobbles during yawn and bounces hard on the clap, ermine scarf jumps on clap, wig curls jostle, coat-tails swing on the forward rock
- **Reference**: Spy (expression-arc mastery) + Moneybags (theatrical delivery). On-brief for "always bored, seeking entertainment."
- **Duration**: 7s
- **Risks to watch in prompt stage**: The eye-dart scan (left→right) needs to stay subtle and slow so it doesn't trigger Seedance camera movement. The clap is the key readable beat — make sure both hands meeting is explicit.

### P5 — Pastry Snack Break
- **Action**: Free hand reaches into the coat, fishes a moment, produces a small golden pastry (mini croissant or petit four). Holds it up, admires it with a delighted smile, darts a **quick guilty glance side-to-side** (no one watching?), then pops it into his mouth. Cheeks puff, eyes close in blissful chew, belly wobbles with a happy hum. A few **golden crumbs** scatter down onto his belly and ermine scarf. Swallows with a satisfied "mmm," brushes crumbs off his belly with the back of his hand (little belly-wobble), slight satisfied belly-pat. Hand returns to hip, canonical.
- **Emotional arc**: Reveal → guilty glance → blissful chew → satisfied sigh → canonical
- **Timing feel**: Quick reveal, fast guilty check, slow blissful savoring, slow crumb-brush. Indulgence with a sneaky comedic beat.
- **VFX element**: Tiny crumb particles falling onto the belly and ermine; small sparkle around the pastry on reveal (it's that good); soft blissful gold glow around closed eyes on the chew
- **Secondary motion**: Belly wobble on chew and crumb-brush, ermine gets dusted with crumbs, wig curls bob with the chew rhythm
- **Reference**: Spy (guilty-glance beat structure) + Princess Sweet (nibbling bliss energy) — fits "indulgent, generous-to-self, jolly" perfectly
- **Duration**: 7s
- **Risks to watch in prompt stage**: Keep the pastry clearly distinct from the croissant scepter so I2V doesn't try to have him bite the scepter head. Specify "small pastry pulled from coat" + "scepter stays upright in other hand."

## Idle Movement Concepts

> Overall idle direction: Fat King is **heavy, content, perpetually amused with himself, slightly bored**. No tense energy — this is a man enjoying his own company. Motion style is slow wobbling/rolling.

### I1 — Belly-Pat Breathing
- **Subtle action**: Relaxed breathing visibly moves the belly in and out. On the second breath, the free hand gives the belly **two soft satisfied pats** before resting back on hip. Tiny approving eyebrow twitch. Content smile throughout.
- **Expression**: Warm content smile, pleased eyes
- **Breathing/weight**: Heavy slow breathing, fully grounded, scepter hand steady
- **Secondary motion**: Belly rises/falls with breath, ermine shifts subtly, wig curls drift, coat-tails settle
- **Duration**: 4s
- **Why it works**: Closest to the client brief Hero Screen description — clean, character-defining, easy for I2V

### I2 — Bored Royal Sigh
- **Subtle action**: Chin rises slightly, long weary sigh escapes, eyes roll upward ("is this *all* there is?"), one finger taps the top of the croissant scepter head absentmindedly — tap, tap, tap — eyes come back level. Small shoulder shrug. Content smile slowly returns. Subtle weight shift once.
- **Expression**: Weary bored → mild eyeroll → reset to content
- **Breathing/weight**: Slow heavy sigh-breathing; slight weight shift onto scepter leg
- **Secondary motion**: Belly lifts on the sigh-inhale and drops on the sigh-release, ermine shifts on shoulder shrug, wig curls drift, scepter sways a micro-degree on the taps
- **Duration**: 4s
- **Why it works**: Nails the "always bored, looking for entertainment" client line in an ambient beat. Pairs especially well with action-forward power concepts (P3/P4).

### I3 — Wig Primp & Preen
- **Subtle action**: Free hand leaves the hip, rises to the side of the powdered wig, **gives a dainty pat-pat to the curls** (adjusting his vanity), fingers briefly touch the crown to seat it straight, hand drifts back down to hip. Tiny self-satisfied eyebrow lift and a pleased smile-widen. Belly breathing throughout.
- **Expression**: Content smile → small vain preen smile → content smile
- **Breathing/weight**: Standard heavy breathing, weight planted on scepter leg during the primp
- **Secondary motion**: Wig curls jiggle under the pat, crown sparkles subtly when touched, ermine shifts on arm raise, belly continues breathing rhythm
- **Duration**: 4s
- **Why it works**: Pure **elegance axis** — realizes the "always elegant" client note. Vanity played warmly, not vainly. Uses head/hair instead of belly for fidget variety.

### I4 — Rolling Sway Wobble
- **Subtle action**: Pure ambient motion. He **shifts weight side to side** in a slow rolling rhythm — left, right, left — belly wobbling with momentum on each shift, scepter tilting a few degrees with the sway like a metronome, wig bobbing gently. No head turn, no expression change — just a happy round king swaying in place. Tiny content smile holds throughout.
- **Expression**: Held content smile, maybe a single slow blink
- **Breathing/weight**: Rolling weight transfer; this IS the breathing substitute
- **Secondary motion**: Belly wobble-lag, ermine scarf swings with body, wig curls bob delayed, scepter tilts with sway, coat-tails drift
- **Duration**: 4s
- **Why it works**: Showcases the **heavy-body / rolling-not-walking motion style** directly, per client brief "Movement Style." Fidget variety: uses whole-body rhythm instead of hand/face. Very Supercell silhouette-level motion.

## Recommendations

### Power
- **P1 (Hearty Belly-Laugh)** — safe, on-brief, direct client signature. Lowest risk, reads as definitively "Fat King."
- **P3 (Royal Coin Shower)** — most ambitious standalone "hero moment" with a signature VFX element. Owns the "generous" beat. Highest production value.
- **P4 (Bored Spots Entertainment)** — best personality arc, uniquely captures the "always bored, seeking entertainment" client note. No other hero does this beat.

### Idle
- **I1 (Belly-Pat Breathing)** — cleanest, safest, pairs with any power. Closest to client Hero Screen brief.
- **I2 (Bored Sigh)** — highest personality load. Best pair for action-heavy powers (P3, P4) since it contrasts with spectacle.

### Strong combos
| Combo | Character read |
|-------|---------------|
| **P4 + I2** | "Bored royal" fully realized — maximum character coherence |
| **P3 + I1** | Generosity spectacle + warm breathing rest |
| **P1 + I4** | The "pure jolly" combo — all wobble all the time |
| **P2 + I3** | The "elegant" combo — theatrical showmanship + vain preen |

## Revised Concepts (2026-04-22 — client weapon-driven rule)

> Appended after client feedback (2026-04-22): power move = weapon action, not ability or personality-only beat. Every hero gets a mandatory breathing-only baseline idle. See `HeroAnimation/Docs/StyleGuide_Animation.md` §2 and `HeroAnimation/PromptTemplates/animation_concepts.md` Core Rules.
>
> **Audit of existing Fat King power concepts against the new rule**:
> - ✅ P2 Croissant Scepter Proclamation — passes (scepter thump + proclamation gesture)
> - ⚠️ P1 Hearty Belly-Laugh — opens with a scepter thump but the main beat is a belly-slap laugh; weapon is incidental. Revised into P6 below.
> - ❌ P3 Royal Coin Shower — no scepter use; throws a coin purse instead. Off-brief as a power move; could be repurposed as an expressive idle variant (I5+) later if desired.
> - ❌ P4 Bored Royal Spots Entertainment — no scepter use; yawn + clap is personality-only. Same note — expressive idle candidate.
> - ❌ P5 Pastry Snack Break — no scepter use; eating a pastry is pure personality. Same note — expressive idle candidate.
>
> **Idle audit**: none of I1-I4 are breathing-only (all add belly pats, sighs, primping, or swaying). I0 below fills the mandatory baseline slot.
>
> **Hand convention reminder**: Fat King holds the scepter in his **anatomical left hand** (viewer's right). Right hand rests on hip. Preserve this in all concepts.

### P6 — Croissant Pole-Arm Stab *(new, direct weapon beat)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (scepter upright in left hand), right hand leaves the hip and joins the left on the scepter shaft — **both hands now on the scepter**. He lifts the scepter slightly, draws it back (short anticipation) and **thrusts the croissant end forward** at chest height with a declarative "HA!" beat, keeping both hands on the shaft. Pulls back, spins the scepter upright in the left hand again, right returns to hip. Settles into canonical with a self-satisfied chuckle.
- **Character delivery**: Regal authority played absurdly — he is delivering a royal thrust with a pastry-on-a-stick. Jolly expression holds through the stab; small "ha!" open-mouthed smile on the thrust peak, contented smile on the settle.
- **Timing feel**: Slow pompous wind-up (~1s), snappy forward stab (~0.3s), slow settle (~2s). Classic Wilhelm-weight timing.
- **VFX**: Faint gold motion streak behind the croissant end during the thrust. Small gold sparkle-pop on the forward peak. No ground impact (this is a forward stab, not a ground slam).
- **Secondary**: Belly leads forward on the thrust then wobbles back on the settle, ermine scarf lifts and resettles, wig curls bounce, coat-tails swing.
- **Reference**: Wilhelm halberd jab structure + client CSV Default Attack Style "Stabbing or smashing with croissant pole-arm."
- **Duration**: 6s

### P7 — Scepter Ground Slam *(new, direct weapon beat)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical, right hand joins left on the scepter. He raises the scepter with both hands **high overhead** (full aristocratic anticipation, chin rises with the lift). Then brings it **straight down** with satisfying weight, croissant end planting solidly onto the ground in front of him. Holds the planted-scepter pose a beat, chest puffed, belly forward. Then lifts the scepter back upright, right hand returns to hip, canonical.
- **Character delivery**: Royal proclamation cadence. Chin-up regal lift → heavy decisive slam → proud-pleased smile on the hold → warm settle.
- **Timing feel**: Heavy slow lift (~1.2s), decisive heavy drop (~0.4s), held plant pose (~1.5s), slow lift back (~1s), settle (~2s). Fat King's motion is the heaviest in the roster.
- **VFX**: Small dust puff where the croissant end hits the ground. **Gold sparkle ring** expanding outward from the scepter on impact (his signature "royal authority" flourish). Crown gleam on the lift peak.
- **Secondary**: Belly and wig curls bounce hard on the ground impact, ermine scarf swings forward on the lift and settles on the slam, coat-tails billow and drop, crown glints on the overhead peak.
- **Reference**: Wilhelm overhead slam structure + client CSV Default Attack Style "smashing with croissant pole-arm."
- **Duration**: 7s

### P8 — Royal Scepter Salute *(new, direct weapon beat)*
- **Weapon action**: From canonical (scepter upright in left hand, right on hip), he brings the scepter to **vertical directly in front of his face** with both hands now gripping the shaft (right briefly leaves the hip to join). The croissant end points straight up at forehead level, gleaming. He tilts his chin up and holds a regal salute pose a beat — pompous, theatrical. Then lowers the scepter in a wide arc down to his side, sweeps it back upright in the left hand, right returns to hip. Ends canonical with a small satisfied nod.
- **Character delivery**: "Elegant" trait made visible — the scepter is treated like a ceremonial saber. Pompous-but-warm, jolly under the aristocratic bearing. Small eyebrow quirk on the salute hold keeps it comedic.
- **Timing feel**: Slow smooth lift (~1.5s), held salute (~1s), wide smooth sweep down (~1.5s), settle (~2s).
- **VFX**: Soft gold shimmer traveling along the scepter shaft during the lift, croissant end glint at the peak of the salute. Subtle aura during the hold.
- **Secondary**: Ermine scarf lifts with arms, wig curls settle as he peaks, belly slowly rises with the deep in-breath, coat-tails drift.
- **Reference**: General sword salute + Count Wilhelm weight; translated into scepter/Fat King register. Realizes the "always elegant" client trait directly.
- **Duration**: 7s

### P9 — Documentary Opener *(one-shot, not a loop, with intentional dialogue)*
- **Purpose**: Replaces the V1 cold-open of the EMTD pipeline mockumentary. Fat King addresses the camera directly with a warm self-introduction. Audio is then voice-cloned in ElevenLabs and used as the narrator throughout the rest of the doc. Only one-shot in the roster — every other concept is a seamless loop.
- **Locked spoken text** (V2): *"Allow me to introduce myself. I am King Ludwig the Round. A few weeks ago, I was a single painted image. Today, I breathe, I smirk, I rule. Let me show you how it was done."* (~28 words, ~9s at 140 wpm). V1 used `swing` instead of `rule` and quoted dialogue in the Action body — both rejected by the text safety filter. V2 strips the quote marks and swaps `swing → rule` to clear the filter.
- **Action**: Begins in the canonical mouth-closed FFLF neutral pose (`Output/Fat_King/Fat_king_FFLF_0.png`). After a ~1.5s settling beat he speaks the line with right-hand presenting / inviting gestures while the croissant scepter stays upright in his left hand throughout. Belly sways gently with each phrase; ermine scarf and wig curls drift with delayed follow-through. Closes back on the canonical neutral pose at t=15s so the FFLF first/last frame match.
- **Character delivery**: Warm, slightly proud, unhurried. Not zany. Voice timbre is the documentary's voice (cloned downstream), so the take must read calm + warm + middle-aged.
- **Timing feel**: ~1.5s pre-speech settle → ~9s speech across five timecoded gesture beats → ~2s post-speech settle into the FFLF closing pose. Total 15s.
- **Rule-flips from standard hero prompts**: (a) literal quoted speech inserted into the Action body — per the 2026-04-28 Architect P10 finding, this is the lever that cues Seedance's audio model to render the actual spoken line; (b) Sound field invites a `warm proud calm middle-aged male speaking voice` instead of banning it; (c) `no music` ban is kept so the cloning sample is dialogue-only. All other Fat King constraints stay (anatomical hands, ermine scarf not mantle, sways not rolls, no blue rim light, etc.).
- **VFX**: None. Clean character + chroma green only — anything else dirties the audio cloning sample.
- **FFLF**: `Fat_king_FFLF_0.png` used as both Start Frame and End Frame.
- **Aspect**: 1:1
- **Duration**: 15s (Seedance hard ceiling)
- **Prompt file**: `Prompts/Seedance/P9_Documentary_Opener.md`

### I0 — Breathing Baseline *(mandatory)*
- **Subtle action**: Canonical pose preserved exactly. Slow deep belly rise/fall with breath. Tiny weight shift between breaths. Nothing else — no belly pats, no sighs, no primping, no sway.
- **Expression**: Held warm content smile. Occasional slow blink.
- **Breathing/weight**: Heavy slow breathing visible at the belly, fully grounded stance, right hand on hip unchanged, left hand holding scepter upright absolutely still.
- **Secondary motion**: Ermine scarf drifts minimally with breath, wig curls rest in place, coat-tails settled.
- **Duration**: 4s
- **Why added**: Satisfies the client mandatory-baseline idle rule. I1-I4 remain as expressive variants on top of this baseline.
