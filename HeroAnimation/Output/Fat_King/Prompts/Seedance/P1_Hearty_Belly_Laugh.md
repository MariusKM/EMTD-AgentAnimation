## Notes for this concept

- This is the **client brief Hero Screen signature** — both hands pat the belly while he wobble-laughs. Lowest risk, highest on-brand read.
- The **three pat-beats on the belly** are the core rhythm — not one limp pat, not continuous beating. Pat-pat-pat with tiny held beats between.
- The **left hand keeps gripping the golden croissant scepter upright throughout** — the scepter must never float or be planted on its own. Only the **right hand** leaves the hip to pat the belly (single-handed belly pat).
- This is a one-handed belly pat; the client brief's "both hands on stomach" is adapted to preserve the canonical scepter-hold (otherwise the scepter reads as floating).
- **Belly wobble is the whole point** — the belly should visibly jiggle/bounce from the pat impacts and from the laughter, lagging slightly behind his body motion.
- Wig curls and ermine scarf jiggle as secondary motion — Supercell-scale jiggle, not a shake.
- Head rocks back once or twice during the laugh, not a rapid chain of snaps — avoids wig/ermine morphing risk.
