# P5 — Pastry Snack Break (Seedance 2.0)

> Power movement | 7s | Static locked-off I2V | Source image = `Source/Heroes Stylized/Fat_king.png`

## Notes for this concept

- The pastry is **NOT gold/metallic** — it's a freshly-baked buttery pastry with a glossy honey-glazed sheen and flaky golden-brown crust. A tiny specular highlight/glint catches on the glaze to signal "unmistakably tasty" without reading as metal.
- **Critical risk**: the pastry must be visually and spatially **separate** from the golden croissant-shaped scepter. The scepter stays upright in the left hand the entire time; the pastry is a small palm-sized treat pulled from inside the coat with the right hand. No biting the scepter.
- **Guilty glance side-to-side** before the bite is the personality beat — "is anyone watching?" with a mischievous smirk. Slow drowsy glances not snappy snaps.
- **Cheek-puff blissful chew** with closed eyes is the savoring payoff. Belly wobbles with the happy hum.
- A few small buttery crumbs scatter onto his belly and the white ermine scarf; he brushes them off with a tiny satisfied belly-pat as the recovery beat.
- Single bite and single chew-savor, not repeated — keeps readability clean.
