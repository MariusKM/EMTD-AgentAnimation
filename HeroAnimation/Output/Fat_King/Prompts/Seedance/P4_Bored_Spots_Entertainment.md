# P4 — Bored Royal Spots Entertainment (Seedance 2.0)

> Power movement | 7s | Static locked-off I2V | Source image = `Source/Heroes Stylized/Fat_king.png`

## Notes for this concept

- Best personality arc in the concept set — **bored → listless scan → sudden delight → clap-belly-laugh → warm settle**. Captures client's "always bored, looking for entertainment" note exactly.
- **Eye scan must be slow and drowsy**, not sharp snappy looks. Half-lidded bored drift left, hold, drift right, hold. Slow eyes, not snaps.
- The **clap** is the key readable beat — both hands meet in front of his chest, belly bounces sharply on the clap impact. Only moment where free hand leaves hip.
- The **scepter stays upright in his left hand** during the clap — the left hand meets the right hand in front of the chest while still holding the scepter, so the scepter tilts forward briefly with his chest forward-rock, then returns upright.
- Small warm gold sparkle-pop above his head on the lightbulb "spot" beat — signals the emotional pivot.
- Head movement on the "spot" should be a single sharp but small head-lift (eyebrows shoot up), not a big head-snap, to avoid wig/ermine morphing.
