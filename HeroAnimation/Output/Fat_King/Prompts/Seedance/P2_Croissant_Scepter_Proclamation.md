- Two-beat structure: **pompous royal build-up → jolly wink payoff**. The comedy is entirely in the contrast between grand bearing and the warm fourth-wall wink at the end.

- The wink is the key personality beat — single eye-close, warm smile widens, tiny belly-bounce chuckle. Must be gentle not frantic.
- Avoid describing "leans forward toward viewer" — say "chest puffs and belly pushes forward" to keep it character-internal and not trigger camera zoom.
- Single held beat at peak is enough — no rapid chain of poses.
