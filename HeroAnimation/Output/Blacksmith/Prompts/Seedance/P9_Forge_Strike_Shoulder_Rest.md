## Notes for this concept

- Weapon-primary rework of old P2. The **forge strikes** are the hero beat; the shoulder-rest + sweat-wipe is the earned recovery.
- **Three rhythmic forward hammer strikes at waist height** (not overhead), each producing a bright spark burst. Sustained clear action per Seedance learnings — prefer three visible strikes over a vague "hammering" read.
- Sparks must be **orange / yellow / warm gold** — never green on the green chroma (keying loss).
- Heavy timing throughout: ~0.5s anticipation + ~0.3s strike per beat, ~1s lift to shoulder, ~1.5s recovery.
- Hammer is held two-handed during the strikes, transitions to right-shoulder rest with the free left hand wiping the brow.
- No running, no walking, no stepping. All action stays inside the canonical silhouette.
