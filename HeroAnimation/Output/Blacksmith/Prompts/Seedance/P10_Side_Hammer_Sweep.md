## Notes for this concept

- Gives the team a **horizontal-strike option** alongside P1's vertical overhead slam — different weapon axis, same stoic strongman register.
- **Wind-up and sweep are slow and heavy, not a flourish**. Diana-slash structure scaled to Wilhelm-weight timing.
- The hammer's trajectory is: right-side wind-up → forward sweep across body → settles tip-down on the left (viewer's right).
- No impact / ground slam — this is a **horizontal pass**, not a strike. Only a faint motion streak + small dust puff where the hammer grounds at the end.
- No sparks (there's nothing to strike) — keep the VFX quiet.
- Avoid the phrase "leans forward toward viewer" (Seedance reads as camera zoom) — use "shifts his weight forward" or "rotates his torso into the sweep."
